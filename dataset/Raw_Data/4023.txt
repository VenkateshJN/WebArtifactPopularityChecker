New BCCI regime to hold working committee meeting on April 14-15 The author has posted comments on this article Gaurav Gupta , TNN | Apr 3 , 2015 , 09.11AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : The new regime of the Board of Control for Cricket in India ( BCCI ) will hold its first working committee meeting in Kolkata on April 14-15 , it is learnt . The board is likely to constitute the new governing council of the IPL , which would have already begun almost a week earlier , at this meeting . The IPL GC was supposed to have been formed at the AGM of the BCCI on March 2 , but the exercise was delayed. Apparently , current BCCI president Jagmohan Dalmiya has been handed two separate lists by the pro and anti-N Srinivasan groups regarding the names in the GC . The technical committee of the BCCI will meet on April 7 in Kolkata. The committee is likely to consider recommendations of captains and coaches . Amroha : Mohammad Shami , who won plaudits for his sterling show at the World Cup recently , has gladdened the hearts of villagers in his native Shaspur Alinagar by buying 40 bighas of land in the village for building a cricket academy for underprivileged children . On Tuesday , Shami signed a deal to purchase the 11 bighas of land from farmers in his village in Amroha district , according to his lawyer .
