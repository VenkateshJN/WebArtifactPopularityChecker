Niall O'Brien Ireland Niall O ’ Brien ’ s contribution in the 2007 and 2011 World Cups has been key in raising Ireland ’ s profile as a cricket nation. Son of Brendan , and elder brother to Kevin and younger to Ciara – all cricketers , O ’ Brien has so far effected 13 dismissals in 15 matches. He is Ireland ’ s best batsman in the tournament with 421 runs , including half-centuries against Pakistan , England and the Netherlands . Best performance ? A calm 72 in a three-wicket win against Pakistan in Jamaica in 2007 .
