( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 LONDON : Team India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni on Sunday ruled out his resignation after his team 's capitulation against England . " I was asked the same question in 2011. Wait and watch , I am strong enough. And if I am not , you will get to know , " he stated . " The lowest point was 2011 because we had the resources then and yet we had failed , " said Dhoni. Asked whether Indian players would be willing to skip IPL for a stint in the English County circuit , Dhoni shot back : " Ask the BCCI about it. And do n't get jealous of IPL. " He added : " Why we are not lasting more than one session is very difficult to answer now. The batting form kept sliding and we were never really able to accumulate the runs to compete.
