World Cup 2015 : President congratulates Indian cricket team The author has posted comments on this article Feb 22 , 2015 , 05.30PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : President Pranab Mukherjee on Sunday congratulated the Indian cricket team after the victory over South Africa in the World Cup in Melbourne . India defeated South Africa by 130 runs at the Melbourne Cricket Ground to break their World Cup jinx of never winning against the Proteas in the mega-event . " Congratulations on your second victory in ICC World Cup 2015 ; the first ever win against S.Africa in World Cup matches , " President Mukherjee tweeted soon after the team 's victory .
