CSK elect to bowl against RCB , Nehra gets another game PTI | May 24 , 2014 , 03.59 PM IST Chennai Super Kings decided to field after winning the toss against Royal Challengers Bangalore. ( PTI Photo ) RELATED BANGALORE : Chennai Super Kings decided to field after winning the toss against Royal Challengers Bangalore in the 14th and final round IPL group league encounter here on Saturday . Veteran seamer Ashish Nehra , who was dropped after the first game came back into the side along with leggie Samuel Badree. The ones dropped are John Hastings and Pawan Negi . RCB made wholesale changes with Chris Gayle , Muttiah Muralitharan , Ashoke Dinda and Abu Nechim Ahmed also being kept out of the playing XI . The ones replacing them are Ravi Rampaul , Rilee Russouw , Vijay Zol and Shadab Zakati .
