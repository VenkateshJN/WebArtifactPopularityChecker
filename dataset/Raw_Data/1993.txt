( TOI Photo ) RELATED KOLKATA : Kolkata Knight Riders skipper Gautam Gambhir won the toss and put Chennai Super Kings in to bat in their Indian Premier League match on Tuesday . Chennai Super Kings have made two changes to their side , bringing in Ben Hilfenhaus and Faf du Plessis in place of David Hussey and Samuel Badree . For Kolkata , Pat Cummins comes in for Morne Morkel .
