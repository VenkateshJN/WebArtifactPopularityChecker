( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 LONDON : Chasing 319 to win , England reached 105 for four at stumps on day four of the second Test against India at Lord 's , here today . Moeen Ali ( 15 batting ) and Joe Root ( 14 batting ) will resume England 's second innings tomorrow . The first Test at Nottingham had ended in a draw. Brief Scores : India : 295 and 342 in 103.1 overs. ( Murali Vijay 95 , Ravindra Jadeja 68 , Bhuvneshwar Kumar 52 ; Liam Plunkett 3/65 , Ben Stokes 3/51 , Moeen Ali 2/28 ) . England 1st innings : 319 and 105 for four in 46 overs ( Gary Ballance 27 , Alastair Cook 22 , Moeen Ali 15 batting , Joe Root 14 batting , Ishant Sharma 2/13 , Mohd Shami 1/20 , Ravindra Jadeja 1/32 ) .
