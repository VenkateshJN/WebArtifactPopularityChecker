( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 GALLE : Sri Lanka defeated Pakistan by seven wickets in the first Test in Galle on Sunday to take a 1-0 lead in the two-match series . The hosts , set a target of 99 runs in a minimum of 21 overs after Pakistan were bowled out for 180 in their second innings , coasted home in the 17th over in front of 5,000 home fans . Mahela Jayawardene , who is due to retire from Test cricket after the series , opened the innings and scored 26 as the hosts beat fading light and approaching rain to record a good win . Kumar Sangakkara made 21 and skipper Angelo Mathews remained unbeaten on 25 as rain hit Galle soon after the match ended .
