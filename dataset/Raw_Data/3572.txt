Dosi , Hedaoo lead RBI to 9-wicket victory The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Feb 12 , 2015 , 12.03PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Left-arm spinner Manish Dosi and off-spinner Parimal Hedaoo bowled well in tandem to help Reserve Bank of India ( RBI ) notch up an easy nine-wicket victory over Ordnance Factory , Bhandara in the Guzder League ( Institutional Zone ) cricket match at the Kalamna ground . Dosi bagged five wickets , while Hedaoo scalped three as RBI bundled out Bhandara side for mere 75. RBI raced to the target in 13.3 overs by losing a solitary wicket. Niraj Gawande ( 31 ) and Dosi ( 21 ) remained unbeaten . Brief Scores Ordnance Factory , Bhandara 75 in 15 overs ( Manish Dosi 5-5 , Parimal Hedaoo 3-13 , Alhad Gunjal 2-20 ) lost to RBI 76-1 in 13.3 overs ( Niraj Gawande 31 not out , Manish Dosi 21 not out ) by nine wickets .
