4th ODI : India elect to bat against Sri Lanka The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Nov 13 , 2014 , 01.36PM IST Kohli-led India have made three changes to the side. ( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 KOLKATA : Indian captain Virat Kohli won the toss and elected to bat against Sri Lanka in the fourth one-dayer at the Eden Gardens on Thursday . India have made three changes to the side , bringing Rohit Sharma , Karn Sharma and Stuart Binny for Shikhar Dhawan , Ishant Sharma and R Ashwin . Dinesh Chandimal , Lahiru Thirimanne , Shaminda Eranga , Ajantha Mendis have come in for Sri Lanka . Teams : India : Virat Kohli ( Capt. ) , Ajinkya Rahane , Robin Uthappa , Ambati Rayudu , Rohit Sharma , Suresh Raina , Stuart Binny , Axar Patel , Karn Sharma , Umesh Yadav , Dhawal Kulkarni Sri Lanka : Angelo Mathews ( Capt. ) , Tillakaratne Dilshan , Kusal Perera , Dinesh Chandimal , Mahela Jayawardene , Lahiru Thirimanne , Thisara Perera , Seekkuge Prasanna , Nuwan Kulasekara , Shaminda Eranga , Ajantha Mendis .
