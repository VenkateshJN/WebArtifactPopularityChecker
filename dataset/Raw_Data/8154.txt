Martin Guptill Losing three toes in an accident when he was younger was not enough to keep this naturally talented batsman down. Martin Guptill was part of the New Zealand ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup team in 2008 and made his One-Day International debut for the Black Caps in early 2009 against the West Indies in fine fashion , scoring 122. Read More Guptill played eight matches for New Zealand in the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup , with his best knock of an unbeaten 86 coming against Zimbabwe . His career high ODI score is an impressive unbeaten 189 , scored against England in 2013 .
