Punjab win toss , opt to bowl against Delhi PTI | May 19 , 2014 , 07.59 PM IST Delhi Daredevils replaced Ross Taylor , Rahul Shukla , Siddarth Kaul and Shahbaz Nadeem with Murali Vijay , Wayne Parnell , Mohammed Shami , Jaydev Unadkat. ( TOI Photo ) RELATED NEW DELHI : Kings XI Punjab captain George Bailey won the toss and elected to bowl in their IPL match against Delhi Daredevils on Monday . Delhi made four changes with Murali Vijay , Wayne Parnell , Mohammed Shami , Jaydev Unadkat coming in place of Ross Taylor , Rahul Shukla , Siddarth Kaul and Shahbaz Nadeem . Punjab made only one change with Beuran Hendricks coming in for Mitchell Johnson . The Teams : Delhi Daredevils : Kevin Pietersen ( c ) , Murali Vijay , Mayank Agarwal , Dinesh Karthik ( wk ) , JP Duminy , Manoj Tiwary , Kedar Jadhav , Wayne Parnell , Imran Tahir , Mohammed Shami , Jaydev Unadkat . Kings XI Punjab : George Bailey ( c ) , Virender Sehwag , Manan Vohra , Wriddhiman Saha ( wk ) , Glenn Maxwell , David Miller , Akshar Patel , Beuran Hendricks , Shivam Sharma , Rishi Dhawan , Sandeep Sharma.
