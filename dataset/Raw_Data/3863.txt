Kadu hits unbeaten 88 to prop up Vidarbha The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Feb 2 , 2015 , 12.19PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Talented opening batsman Tushar Kadu scored an unbeaten 88 to help Vidarbha make a decent total of 224 for 6 against Chhattisgarh on Day 1 of the Col CK Nayudu U-23 Cricket tournament in Raipur on Sunday . Kadu , who originally hails from Yavatmal , played a patient knock of 88 ( 273b , 8x4 ) to steady the rocking ship of Vidarbha , who opted to bat. Kadu got good support from Jitu Sharma , who also notched up a brilliant half-century with the help of seven fours. The duo of Kadu and Sharma added 96 runs for the fifth wicket . At close , Kadu and Akshay Karnewar ( 33 ) were batting at the crease . Brief Scores : Vidarbha ( 1st innings ) : 224-6 in 88 overs ( Tushar Kadu 88 no , Jitu Sharma 51 , Akshay Karnewar 33 no , R Sanjay 15 ; Mohd Shahnawaz 3-45 , J Gill 2-52 , P Sinha 1-34 ) vs Chhattisgarh .
