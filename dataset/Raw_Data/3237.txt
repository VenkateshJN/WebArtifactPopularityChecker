( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 MELBOURNE : Indian captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni won the toss and chose to bat in the tri-series one-day international against Australia at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on Sunday . Dhoni returns as captain of the India limited overs line-up after retiring from Test cricket during the recent series against Australia . India also named left-arm spinner Akshar Patel as part of a bowling attack containing seamers Umesh Yadav , Mohammed Sami and Bhuvneshwar Kumar . Australia named 21-year-old New South Wales quick Gurinder Sandhu to make his ODI debut . Sandhu , who is not a member of Australia 's World Cup squad , replaces Xavier Doherty in the only change to the line-up that won the tri-series opener against England by three wickets in Sydney on Friday . Sunday 's match brings together the two top-ranked teams in one-day cricket and winners of the last two World Cups . Teams : India : MS Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Shikhar Dhawan , Rohit Sharma , Virat Kohli , Ajinkya Rahane , Suresh Raina , Ravi Ashwin , Akshar Patel , Mohammed Shami , Bhuvneshwar Kumar , Umesh Yadav . Australia : George Bailey ( Capt. ) , David Warner , Aaron Finch , Shane Watson , Steve Smith , Glenn Maxwell , Brad Haddin , James Faulkner , Mitchell Starc , Pat Cummins , Gurinder Sandhu .
