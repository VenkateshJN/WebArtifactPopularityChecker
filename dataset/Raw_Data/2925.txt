READ ALSO : Chris Gayle hits first-ever World Cup double hundred Gayle 's knock was adorned with a record-equalling 16 sixes and 10 fours. Gayle fell short of the highest number of sixes by only one , tying with Rohit and South African captain AB de Villiers . Gayle , however , became the highest individual scorer in a World Cup match , surpassing South African Gary Kirsten 's 188 made in Rawalpindi in the 1996 edition . The destructive batsman was also involved in a record 372-run second-wicket partnership with centurion Marlon Samuels ( 133 ) to help the West Indies post 372 for 2 in 50 overs against Zimbabwe . Sachin Tendulkar had registered one-day cricket 's first 200 in 2010 against South Africa. ( AFP Photo ) The legendary Indian duo of Tendulkar , who played in record six World Cups , and Rahul Dravid held the partnership record which they made against New Zealand at Hyderabad in 1999 . Gayle thus became the only player in world cricket to hit a triple hundred in Tests , double hundred in ODIs and a hundred in Twenty20 Internationals .
