Delhi retain T20 squad for final three matches The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 25 , 2015 , 05.36PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : Delhi have retained their 16-member squad led by Gautam Gambhir for the remaining three games of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy North Zone leg . The selection committee met at the DDCA on Tuesday to finalize the squad for the games against Himachal ( March 26 ) , Haryana ( March 27 ) and Services ( March 28 ) . Delhi have won their first two matches against Jammu and Kashmir and Punjab respectively with senior batsman Virender Sehwag top-scoring in both the games . Gambhir though has struggled to get going , scoring 10 and 4 in the two games so far , and would like to pull up his socks before the Indian Premier League ( IPL ) begins April 8 . Team : Gautam Gambhir ( captain ) , Virender Sehwag , Unmukt Chand , Milind Kumar , Nitish Rana , Kshitiz Sharma , Jagrit Anand , Pratyush Singh , Vaibhav Rawal , Pawan Negi , Varun Sood , Rahul Yadav ( wk ) , Pranshu Vijayran , Parvinder Awana , Pawan Suyal , Aditya Kaushik .
