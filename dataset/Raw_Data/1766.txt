Published on Mar 10 , 2015 On March 5 , South African national team of ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 gave a special coaching clinic at Eden Park , Auckland , to New Zealand youths as part of the THINK WISE initiative. THINK WISE is a partnership between the ICC , UNAIDS and UNICEF that aims to raise awareness around HIV with the power of cricket . The children had the chance to learn about the skills involved with cricket , batting , bowling and fielding , from the players and coach , while also understanding the background of THINK WISE and learning about HIV and AIDS awareness .
