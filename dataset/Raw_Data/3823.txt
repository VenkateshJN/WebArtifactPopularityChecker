International cricket betting racket unearthed by ED The author has posted comments on this article Piyush Mishra , TNN | Mar 20 , 2015 , 02.49PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 In the Rs 500 crore international cricket betting racket unearthed by ED on Thursday , ED revealed that bookies Kiran and Tommy used to do betting through UK based website www.betfair.com and used to send Rs 5 crore per match to each Pakistan and Dubai . They used to be in touch with their counterparts in Pakistan , Dubai and other parts of India through Skype . Also ED recovered 40 live cartridges from Kiran 's home in Maninagar last night .
