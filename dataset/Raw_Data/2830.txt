Rafael Nadal visits Buenos Aires children The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Feb 27 , 2015 , 02.13PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 BUENOS AIRES : Spanish tennis star Rafael Nadal visited a poor neighborhood in Buenos Aires on Thursday and exchanged a few volleys with excited children . In a T-shirt and high socks , the 14-time grand slam champion played on clay courts with a handful of boys and then offered autographs to the 100 children who came out for the event with the star. Nadal also played doubles with the mayor of Buenos Aires , Mauricio Macri . Nadal is in the country for the ATP Argentina Open and he had a victory Wednesday in his opening match . The tennis complex Nadal visited is part of a social program for 12 poor neighborhoods in the city which is known for its love of soccer . Rackets and other equipment for the 300 children aged 6 to 15 who play at the complex are provided by the International Tennis Federation , organizers said .
