( PTI Photo ) Page 1 of 4 RANCHI : Sri Lanka captain Angelo Mathews won the toss and elected to bat against India in the fifth and final cricket one-dayer here on Sunday . India , leading the series 4-0 , have made a couple of changes to the side handing a debut to batsman Kedar Jadhav , who has replaced Suresh Raina . Pacer Umesh Yadav has been rested making way for off-spinner R Ashwin . Teams : India : V Kohli ( c ) , R Sharma , A Rahane , A Rayudu , K Jadhav , R Uthappa , S Binny , A Patel , R Ashwin , K Sharma , D Kulkarni Sri Lanka : A Mathews ( c ) , T Dilshan , N Dickwella , D Chandimal , M Jayawardene , L Thirimanne , N Perera , S Prasanna , R Eranga , A Mendis , P Gamage .
