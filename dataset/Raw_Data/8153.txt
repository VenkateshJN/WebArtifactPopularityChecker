Luke Ronchi Luke Ronchi is a former Australia wicketkeeper who previously played four One-Day Internationals for his birth country in 2008. Read More Since moving to Wellington in 2012 to further his cricketing career , Ronchi has made a name for himself in New Zealand and became eligible to play for the Black Caps in January 2013 . His ODI debut came in May 2013 against England and he now has more than 30 ODI caps to his name for the Black Caps , including three fifties. Not only talented behind the stumps , Ronchi is also an explosive batsman and plays an entertaining brand of cricket .
