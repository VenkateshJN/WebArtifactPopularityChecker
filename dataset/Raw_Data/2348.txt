( TOI Photo ) RELATED MOHALI : Rajasthan Royals won the toss and invited Kings XI Punjab to bat in an Indian Premier League match on Friday . Kings XI Punjab have rested Glenn Maxwell with David Miller and Lakshmipathy Balaji back in the XI while leg-spinner Karanveer Singh is making his IPL debut. Rajasthan Royals have made four changes with Pravin Tambe , Ajinkya Rahane , Rahul Tewatia and Vikramjeet Malik included in the playing XI .
