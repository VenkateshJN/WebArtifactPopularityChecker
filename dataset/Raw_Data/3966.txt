Memorable moments - when Viv had a field day The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 3 , 2015 , 11.52AM IST Page 1 of 4 1975 : WEST INDIES VS AUSTRALIA The West Indian bowlers failed to take Australia 's wicket numbers 2 , 3 and 4 in the title clash. Viv Richards took them with his fielding skills . First , Allan Turner with a direct hit at the striker 's end from the mid-wicket. Turner was late off his blocks from the non-striker 's end. Then , Greg Chappell who ran after an initial 'NO ' with brother Ian due to a misfield in the cover-point region . And finally Ian himself. A left-handed pickup and a right-handed sharp throw to the bowler found the Australian skipper short of his crease. Australia fell short of 291 by 17 runs . Indian fans still rejoice in Kapil Dev 's great catch in the 1983 World Cup final to dismiss Richards. A great piece of fielding always has a potential to change a match.
