Royal Challengers Bangalore opt to bat against Sunrisers Hyderabad PTI | May 20 , 2014 , 04.05 PM IST Royal Challengers Bangalore captain Virat Kohli won the toss and elected to bat against Sunrisers Hyderabad. ( TOI Photo ) RELATED HYDERABAD : Royal Challengers Bangalore captain Virat Kohli won the toss and elected to bat against Sunrisers Hyderabad in their Indian Premier League match on Tuesday . Bangalore are playing an unchanged side , while Parvez Rasool and Venugopal Rao have come in place of Amit Mishra and Lokesh Rahul for Hyderabad .
