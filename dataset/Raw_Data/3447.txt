Boult becomes New Zealand 's top World Cup wicket-taker The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 24 , 2015 , 08.54AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 AUCKLAND : Trent Boult became New Zealand 's top wicket-taker in a World Cup series with two early wickets in their semifinal against South Africa at Eden Park on Tuesday . The dismissals lifted Boult to 21 wickets , the most by any bowler in this World Cup and one ahead of the previous New Zealand best of 20 set by Geoff Allott in 1999 . Boult bowled Hashim Amla for 10 and had Quinton de Kock caught in the deep for 14 . After eight overs South Africa were 31 for two .
