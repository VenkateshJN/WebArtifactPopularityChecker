Bloomberg roots for India in Cricket World Cup The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Feb 16 , 2015 , 05.50PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : In the capital to attend a renewable energy investor meet , New York City 's former Mayor and media baron Michael Bloomberg wants India to lift the Cricket World Cup in Australia . In a light-vein address , Bloomberg also expressed his love for Bollywood. " ... if i get an offer to play a part in Bollywood movie... and come back and collect the best actor trophy. " " I do n't think SRK ( Shah Rukh Khan ) has anything to worry about , " he said in a tongue-in-cheek manner . Talking about cricket , Bloomberg also mentioned about India 's win over Pakistan in the World Cup match yesterday . " I want to see India winning the title second time in row...Go Team Blue ! " Drawing parallels between New York and New Delhi , he said both places are great to find Indian food at 3 in the morning . " We want more number of Indians to study in America and work there as we want more intelligent and smart people. " He said the immigration laws is what the present federal government needs to look at . Before leaving for India , the three-term mayor of New York City had said he would focus on climate leadership , solar power , smart cities , road safety and Indian youth during his trip .
