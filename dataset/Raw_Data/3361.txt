Aussies wary of Indian batting : Faulkner The author has posted comments on this article Santosh Suri , TNN | Jan 18 , 2015 , 11.17AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MELBOURNE : The Australians may have won the Test series against India , but the visitors managed to instill some fear on the dint of their batting form in the four-match series . Talking to the media at the Melbourne Airport after the Aussies arrived from Sydney , James Faulkner said he was pretty impressed by how the Indian batsmen went about their business in the Tests . " It was a very good Test series despite the lop-sided result as the Indian batsmen were in pretty good touch , Virat Kohli was in top form , " said the bowling all-rounder . Faulkner felt that the Indians were likely to carry the same form into their tri-series , but added that he and his bowlers would be ready . " There is no doubting that they are going to be tough tomorrow. It 's going to be a good high-scoring One-day contest.
