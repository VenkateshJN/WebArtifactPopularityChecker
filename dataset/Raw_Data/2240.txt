( PTI Photo ) RELATED ABU DHABI : Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and opted to bat against Kolkata Knight Riders in their IPL 7 encounter on Tuesday . The KKR dropped an out-of-form Yusuf Pathan from the playing XI including Manvinder Bisla. All-rounder Shakib al Hasan also comes into the XI in place of Chris Lynn . Rajasthan Royals included left-arm spinner Iqbal Abdullah and also brought back James Faulkner while Abhishek Nayar and Tim Southee will sit out .
