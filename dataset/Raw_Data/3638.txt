Ganguly hails Dalmiya 's return as BCCI chief The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 3 , 2015 , 07.53PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 KOLKATA : Former India captain Sourav Ganguly on Tuesday hailed the return of Jagmohan Dalmiya as BCCI president and said the seasoned cricket administrator will fulfil all the expectations . " He knows ( things ) better than anybody else. He will do all the right things. He will fulfil all the expectations , " Ganguly said . There were reports about Dalmiya 's desire for Ganguly to head the BCCI technical committee but the former skipper refused to comment of the issue . " I will see when it comes , " Ganguly said. Dalmiya taking over BCCI to job may also turn out to be a windfall of sorts for the Cricket Association of Bengal which is expecting to host the final of World Twenty20 next year . " We expect to get big matches for the next World Cup including a bid for the final , " an official said , adding that the first Working Committee meeting of the Board after the election of new office bearers may be held in the third week of this month here .
