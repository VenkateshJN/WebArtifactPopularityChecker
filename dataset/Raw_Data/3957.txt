MCG crowd record for ICC World Cup final The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 29 , 2015 , 12.59PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MELBOURNE : The World Cup final between Australia and New Zealand at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on Sunday has broken the venue 's previous record attendance for a cricket match of 91,112 , according to officials . The official attendance will be announced later in the day , but it has already surpassed the biggest cricket crowd at the vast stadium , set during the 2013 Boxing Day Ashes Test against England . Sunday 's crowd has also topped the attendance of 87,182 for the 1992 World Cup final at the MCG between Pakistan and England .
