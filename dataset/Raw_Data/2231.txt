( TOI Photo ) RELATED KOLKATA : Kings XI Punjab skipper George Bailey won the toss and put Kolkata Knight Riders in to bat in the Qualifier 1 of the Indian Premier League on Wednesday . While Punjab named an unchanged eleven , Kolkata brought leg-spinner Piyush Chawla in place of speedster Vinay Kumar .
