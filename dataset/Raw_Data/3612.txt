Fans want Dhoni to be around till 2019 World Cup The author has posted comments on this article Shweta Singh , TNN | Mar 27 , 2015 , 11.16AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 AHMEDABAD : India 's ODI captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni may have left open the possibility of playing in the 2019 World Cup in England , saying that he would take a call after the Twenty20 World Cup next year , but Ahmedabad fans have put their weight behind the skipper and urged that he should be around till then . Dhoni , who retired from Test cricket late last year , said that he is 33 now and will wait till the T20 World Cup , to be held in India next March. Dhoni is rated among the best finishers in ODI cricket right now . Abhinav Sinha said that Dhoni is still the best finisher for India and that the team needs him. " I do n't have doubt on his abilities. He is just 33 and should stick around for 3-4 years , " he said . Terming Dhoni as one of the fittest in the Indian team , Shubham Desai that Dhoni 's success in ODIs makes him a sort after player. " He is leading from the front , batting well down the order and staying cool in tough situations. I want him to play the next WC , " the 19-year-old student said .
