Sachin Tendulkar on Google + hangout Sunday The author has posted comments on this article IANS | Feb 21 , 2015 , 08.51PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : Batting legend Sachin Tendulkar will go live on social networking app Google + hangout on Sunday during the mid-innings break of the India vs South Africa World Cup Pool B encounter at the Melbourne Cricket Ground ( MCG ) . The initiative is being orchestrated by the International Cricket Council ( ICC ) in collaboration with Google . The conversation can be followed on Google + with # AskSachin . Tendulkar is the brand ambassador of the 2015 World Cup .
