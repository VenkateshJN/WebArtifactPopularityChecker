Dharmasena , Kettleborough named umpires for World Cup final The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 27 , 2015 , 01.16PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 DUBAI : Sri Lanka 's Kumar Dharmasena and England 's Richard Kettleborough were on Friday appointed on-field umpires for the ICC Cricket World Cup final between Australia and New Zealand on Sunday in Melbourne . " Kumar Dharmasena and Richard Kettleborough will be the two on-field umpires , " the ICC said in a statement . Sri Lanka 's Ranjan Madugalle was named the match referee for the high-octane clash between the trans-Tasmanian rivals. While Australia beat India by 95 runs in their semifinal clash , New Zealand got the better of South Africa in their last-four stage match . The match official appointments are as follows : March 29 : Australia v New Zealand , Melbourne. Ranjan Madugalle ( match referee ) , Kumar Dharmasena and Richard Kettleborough ( both on-field umpires ) , Marais Erasmus ( third umpire ) , Ian Gould ( fourth umpire ) .
