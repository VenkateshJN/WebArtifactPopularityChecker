Bravo set to miss rest of IPL owing to shoulder injury PTI | Apr 21 , 2014 , 09.17 PM IST READ MORE Ravichandran Ashwin | Mahendra Singh Dhoni | IPL | Dwayne Bravo | Chennai Super Kings RELATED ABU DHABI : Chennai Super Kings all-rounder Dwayne Bravo is set to miss the rest of ongoing edition of the Indian Premier League due to a shoulder injury , dealing a blow to the two-time champions as the West Indian has been integral part of the side . Ahead of today 's match against Delhi Daredevils , CSK captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni said that Bravo could miss " 4 to 5 weeks " of IPL 7 after a bad fall in the last game . " Sad that he wo n't be part of the dressing room , hope he recovers soon , " Dhoni said . Bravo had suffered the injury while fielding during Kings XI Punjab 's innings last Friday . Bravo took a tumble while fielding at point region in the 11th over to get himself injured . He unsuccessfully tried to stop a switch hit by Glenn Maxwell off Ravichandran Ashwin in the last delivery of the 11th over with a diving effort and injured his shoulder in the process .
