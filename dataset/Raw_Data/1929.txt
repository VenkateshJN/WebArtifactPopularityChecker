( PTI Photo ) RELATED BANGALORE : Delhi Daredevils captain Kevin Pietersen won the toss and elected to bowl in their IPL match against Royal Challengers Bangalore on Tuesday . Delhi brought in Murali Vijay in place of Laxmi Shukla while Bangalore made three changes with Muttiah Muralitharan , Sachin Rana and Abu Nechim replacing Albie Morkel , Varun Aaron and Ashok Dinda respectively . The match , which was delayed by one hour and 10 minutes , will be a full 20-over-a-side affair .
