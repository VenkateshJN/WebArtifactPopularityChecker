( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 SYDNEY : Captain Virat Kohli on Thursday became the second Indian batsman to score 500 runs in a Test series in Australia during the fourth and final match at the SCG . Kohli , who came into the match with 499 runs from the three earlier matches of the series , reached that landmark by hitting a four of the bowling of off-spinner Nathan Lyon during the morning session in the 44th over of India 's first innings . The 26-year-old Kohli , who was playing in his 33rd Test and seventh innings in this series , followed in the footsteps of batting legend Rahul Dravid who accumulated 619 runs from eight innings in the 2003-04 Australia tour . Kohli , who made his Test debut in 2011 against the West Indies , has already hit three centuries in the series .
