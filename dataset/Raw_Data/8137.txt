Read More He made his one-day international debut against West Indies in 2010 and has since become an important part of the Australian line-up . Smith played six matches in the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup with a top score of 25 . His leg-spin has also been a handy ace up the sleeve of skipper Michael Clarke , and Smith himself has shown his leadership qualities in recent times . There ’ s no doubting Smith is in form and will be a key part of Australia ’ s World Cup plans. From 12 ODI innings in 2014 , Smith scored two centuries – 101 off 118 balls against Pakistan and 104 from 112 balls against South Africa – to finish the year with 541 one-day runs .
