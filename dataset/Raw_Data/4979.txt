Sri Lanka tour of India , 2014 Vinay Kumar , Robin Uthappa selected for last 2 ODIs Mumbai , 2014/Nov/10 04 : 04 : 00 Text Size India have already won the ODI series by winning the first three games © Agencies Vinay Kumar , Robin Uthappa , Rohit Sharma , Karn Sharma and Kedar Jadhav have been brought in to the Indian squad for the last 2 ODIs against Sri Lanka. Shikhar Dhawan , Wriddhiman Saha , Ishant Sharma , Ravindra Jadeja , Amit Mishra and Murali Vijay are the players who have been removed from the 15-man squad that was selected for the first 3 ODIs . MS Dhoni , who was initially rested for 3 ODIs , has been given an extended break to recuperate from his forearm injury ahead of the tour to Australia . India have already won the five-match ODI series 3-0 , with thumping wins in Cuttack , Ahmedabad and Hyderabad . 14-man squad for 4th and 5th ODIs against Sri Lanka : Virat Kohli ( C ) , Ajinkya Rahane , Rohit Sharma , Ambati Rayudu , Suresh Raina , Robin Uthappa , Axar Patel , Karn Sharma , R Ashwin , Umesh Yadav , Dhawal Kulkarni , Stuart Binny , Vinay Kumar , Kedar Jadhav . © Cricbuzz
