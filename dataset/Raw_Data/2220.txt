( PTI Photo ) RELATED RANCHI : Chennai Super Kings skipper MS Dhoni won the toss and opted to bat in their Indian Premier League ( IPL ) match against Kolkata Knight Riders on Friday . The match has been reduced to 17-overs-a-side contest after a 1 hour 40 minutes delay due to rain . Chennai are playing an unchanged side while Kolkata have made two changes , bringing in Andre Russell for Morne Morkel , and Yusuf Pathan for Manvinder Bisla .
