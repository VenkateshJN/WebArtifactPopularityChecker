( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 MIRPUR : The third and final One-day International between India and Bangladesh was reduced to 40 over a side after rain halted play at the Shere-e-Bangla Stadium on Thursday . India , who have already won the series by winning the first two games , were 37 for four in 12.3 overs when rain disturbed proceedings . The play will resume at 4.30 p.m. India time .
