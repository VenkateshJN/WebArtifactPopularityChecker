Rajasthan Royals invite Hyderabad to bat PTI | May 8 , 2014 , 07.51 PM IST Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad. ( TOI Photo ) RELATED AHMEDABAD : Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in an IPL match at Sardar Patel Stadium in Ahmedabad on Thursday . Hyderabad have included Moises Henriques and Amit Mishra in place of Darren Sammy and Ishant Sharma . Rajasthan have also made two changes , bringing in Dhawal Kulkarni and Kane Richardson by dropping Rahul Tewatia and Tim Southee .
