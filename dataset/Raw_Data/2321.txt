Mumbai Indians put into bat , Harbhajan back in playing XI PTI | May 23 , 2014 , 04.15 PM IST Mumbai Indians were put into bat by Delhi Daredevils skipper Kevin Pietersen in a do-or-die game. ( TOI Photo ) RELATED MUMBAI : Mumbai Indians were put into bat by Delhi Daredevils skipper Kevin Pietersen in a do-or-die game in the seventh edition of Indian Premier League on Friday . Harbhajan Singh comes back into the playing XI after a break of one match while Merchant de Lange gets his first game of the tournament. Krishmar Santokie and Pragyan Ojha were dropped from the playing XI . For Dardedevils , Shahbaz Nadeem and Siddharth Kaul got a look-in in the playing XI in place of Mohammed Shami and Mayank Agarwal .
