( PTI Photo ) RELATED SHARJAH : Royal Challengers Bangalore won the toss and elected to bowl in their Indian Premier League ( IPL ) match against Kolkata Knight Riders on Thursday . RCB have made two changes to the playing XI , bringing in Muttiah Muralitharan and Yogesh Takawale for Nic Maddinson and Sachin Rana . KKR are playing Umesh Yadav for Piyush Chawla and Chris Lynn for Shakib Al Hasan respectively .
