World Cup warm-up match : Australia opt to bat against India The author has posted comments on this article AP | Feb 8 , 2015 , 09.09AM IST Bailey is leading the Australian side despite Clarke being in the team. ( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 ADELAIDE : Australian captain George Bailey won the toss and chose to bat in their World Cup warm-up match against India at the Adelaide Oval on Sunday . Rohit Sharma was passed fit to play but Ishant Sharma withdrew from India 's World Cup squad with a knee injury . Shane Watson returned for Australia after recent hamstring problems . The two teams will feature all their fifteen players in the encounter that falls just a week before the main event that begins on February 14 . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Ajinkya Rahane , Shikhar Dhawan , Ambati Rayudu , Rohit Sharma , Virat Kohli , Suresh Raina , Stuart Binny , Ravi Ashwin , Ravi Jadeja , Axar Patel , Mohammed Shami , Mohit Sharma , Buvneshwar Kumar , Umesh Yadav . Australia : George Bailey ( Capt. ) , Aaron Finch , David Warner , Michael Clarke , Shane Watson , Steve Smith , Mitchell Marsh , Glenn Maxwell , Brad Haddin , Mitchell Johnson , Mitchell Starc , Josh Hazlewood , Xavier Doherty , Pat Cummins .
