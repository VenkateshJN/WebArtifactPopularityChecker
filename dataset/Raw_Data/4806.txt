World Cup 2015 : Shafiul cleared to join Bangladesh squad The author has posted comments on this article AP | Feb 23 , 2015 , 03.16PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MELBOURNE : The International Cricket Council has approved Shafiul Islam as a replacement in Bangladesh 's World Cup squad for pace bowler Al-Amin Hossain , who was sent home after breaking a team curfew . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 Al-Amin did not play in the team 's opening 105-run win over Afghanistan , and Bangladesh 's second match against Australia was washed out in Brisbane on the weekend . The ICC issued a statement Monday saying Shafiul , who has played 52 one-day internationals for Bangladesh , had been approved to join the 15-man squad in " an exceptional circumstance.'' Bangladesh is due to play 1996 champion Sri Lanka on Thursday .
