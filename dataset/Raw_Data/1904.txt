( TOI Photo ) RELATED MUMBAI : Royal Challengers Bangalore skipper Virat Kohli won the toss and invited Mumbai Indians to bat first in an Indian Premier League match on Tuesday . RCB are unchanged while Mumbai have brought in Pawan Suyal in place of an injured Zaheer Khan , who has been ruled out for the remainder of the tournament .
