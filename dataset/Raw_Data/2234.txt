( TOI Photo ) RELATED DUBAI : Kolkata Knight Riders skipper Gautam Gambhir won the toss and elected to bat against Delhi Daredevils in an IPL Twenty20 cricket match at Dubai International Cricket Stadium , here on Saturday . While KKR are unchanged , Delhi have made two changes to their team , bringing Nathan Coulter-Nile and Jaydev Unadkat in for Wayne Parnell and Rahul Sharma .
