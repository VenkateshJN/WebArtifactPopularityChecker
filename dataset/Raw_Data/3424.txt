( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : Off-spinner Ravichandran Ashwin , who won the Cricket Board 's Polly Umrigar Trophy best player award in 2012-13 season , has been recommended by the BCCI for the Government 's prestigious Arjuna Award this year . " Yes , Ashwin has been recommended for the Arjuna Award before the April 30 deadline. No other cricketer has been recommended ( for the awards ) , " said Ratnakar Shetty , BCCI General Manager ( Game Development ) . The cricketer to get the Arjuna Award was Virat Kohli last year. A total of 46 cricketers , including women , have been conferred the sporting honour . Ashwin became the fastest bowler in 80 years to complete 100 wickets in his 19th Test last November against the West Indies at the Wankhede Stadium here , the farewell game of Sachin Tendulkar . The 27-year-old Chennai-born tweaker overtook the previous Indian record of one of the members of the famed spin quartet - off-spinner Erapalli Prasanna - to reach the 100- wicket mark in one fewer Test . Ashwin , who is also a useful batsman , has taken 104 wickets in Tests , 106 in 79 ODIs and 25 wickets in the same number of T20 internationals .
