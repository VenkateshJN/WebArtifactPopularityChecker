Govt challenges HC ban on ICC World Cup broadcast The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 10 , 2015 , 09.37AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : In a bid to ensure that people are not deprived of watching the cricket World Cup , the Centre on Monday moved the Supreme Court challenging a Delhi high court order barring Prasar Bharati from sharing with cable operators the live feed on which ESPN and Star Sports have exclusive broadcasting rights . Appearing before a bench of Chief Justice HL Dattu and Justice AK Sikri , attorney general Mukul Rohatgi urged the court for an urgent hearing as the World Cup starts from February 13 . He submitted that lakhs of people , for whom cricket is a religion , would not be able to watch the games due to the HC order. The case will be heard on Tuesday .
