Mujumdar Club , MSSC post easy wins in DRM Challenge Cup Cricket The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Feb 24 , 2015 , 03.00PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Mujumdar Cricket Club and MSSC registered comfortable victories in the Haier Twenty20 DRM Challenge Cup Cricket tournament at the Central Railway Ajni ground on Monday . While Mujumdar CC downed Brotherhood Club , Wardha by 44 runs , MSSC got the better of Combined Department XI by 7 wickets . Brief Scores : Combined Department 113-8 in 20 overs ( Sudhir Pendam 18 , Shankar Nandeliya 17 , Pawan Deshmukh 13 ; Jitesh Sharma 4-13 , Bhushan Talmade 2-22 ) lost to MSSC 116-3 in 9.1 overs ( Abhishek Choudhary 63 not out , Y Tembhare 23 ; Nitin Ahirrao 2-18 ) by 7 wickets . Man-of-the-match : Abhishek Choudhary . Mujumdar Cricket Club 159-7 in 20 overs ( Ankit Tiwari 54 , Bharat Singh 25 , Pawan Mendule 18 not out ; Shubham Lohakare 2-31 , Pawan Thakur 2-38 ) beat Brotherhood Club , Wardha 115-8 in 20 overs ( Sumit Meshram 25 , Gaurav Thakur 24 ; Ankit Tiwari 2-13 , Vedant Ingle 2-17 ) by 44 runs .
