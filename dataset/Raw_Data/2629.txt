( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 BULAWAYO ( Zimbabwe ) : Zimbabwe beat Afghanistan by six wickets in the first of four one-day internationals on Friday . Sean Williams ' 70 from 65 balls set up the host 's victory as Zimbabwe reached 227-4 to overtake Afghanistan 's 223-9 with nearly five overs to spare . Williams shared a 93-run stand with captain Brendan Taylor ( 43 ) for one of two decisive partnerships. Williams then put on 85 with Elton Chigumbura before falling with his team just short of the target . Opener Sikandar Raza returned after retiring hurt earlier in the innings to see the Zimbabweans home alongside Chigumbura . Playing its first bilateral series against a test-playing nation , Afghanistan 's total was set up by half centuries from Samiullah Shenwari , who made 65 not out , and captain Mohammad Nabi , who contributed 54 from just 32 balls .
