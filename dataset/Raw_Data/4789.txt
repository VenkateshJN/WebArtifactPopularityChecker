In February , during a press launch of his autobiography , Playing It My Way , Sachin Tendulkar was asked who will win the 2015 Cricket World Cup . Full coverage : ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 He replied , " I ca n't pin-point at one team but there are a few competitive teams. I would like to name Australia , South Africa , New Zealand and India " . Intriguingly , his predictions have been spot on . While he labelled New Zealand as 'dark horses ' , he said that India could surprise other teams with some quality spin . Master blaster made this prediction when Indian cricket team was facing a lot of criticism following their poor show in Australia in Tests and the tri-series . Interestingly , during the event , he had also ruled out any chances of England qualifying for the semifinals . Well , 1.2 billion Indians call him 'god of Cricket ' for a reason .
