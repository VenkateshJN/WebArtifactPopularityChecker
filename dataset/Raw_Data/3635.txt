Game changer - Brendan Taylor : Made to measure The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 4 , 2015 , 11.27AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 If you happen to stumble upon Brendan Taylor 's Facebook page , one particular post gets your immediate attention -a photo of him slamming a South African bowler over mid-wicket , with AB de Villiers ' worried eyes following the ball from first slip. The caption to the picture , which Taylor claims is n't his , reads : " Just missed it but 93 is n't bad I guess. " In a team of no-hopers , Taylor is one Zimbabwean who has managed to look the opposition in the eye. He has struck six hundreds and nine half-hundreds in his 161-match ODI career. Add to that the fact that he keeps wickets and if not that , he can chip in with some handy off-spin . Taylor 's highest ODI score -145 -came against a quality South African attack at Bloemfontein in 2010. He has also notched up centuries against New Zealand , Sri Lanka and Zimbabwe 's perennial sparring partner Bangladesh .
