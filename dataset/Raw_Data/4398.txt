Vettori heroics prevent logistics nightmare for Dhoni & co The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Mar 14 , 2015 , 08.31AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 AUCKLAND : As Bangladesh were fighting New Zealand in Hamilton , there were some anxious faces in Auckland. If Bangladesh had won the game , India would have had to travel to Sydney to play Sri Lanka in the quarterfinal , and not their eastern neighbours in Melbourne . Just as the Indian journos were worried about their schedules going topsy-turvy , the manager of the Indian team too was feeling the heat. Even as the Indian players were happy being at the gym or chilling by the poolside , the two managers - Dr RN Baba and logistics manager Sathish - were huddled together hoping New Zealand would scrape through. " The players are cool , they are ready to play any team. But the problem is ours. If Bangladesh win , all the tickets that have been booked for Melbourne will have to be changed to Sydney , " Dr Baba said . Thankfully , Daniel Vettori saved the day for India 's managers .
