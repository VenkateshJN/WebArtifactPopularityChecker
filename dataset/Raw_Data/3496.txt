Coetzer hits Scotland 's first World Cup century The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 5 , 2015 , 09.51AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NELSON ( New Zealand ) : Opening batsman Kyle Coetzer became the first Scottish player to make a World Cup century on Thursday in the game against Bangladesh . Coetzer reached the landmark in style with a huge six , his second of the innings at Saxton Oval . It was the 30-year-old Coetzer 's second ODI century having also scored a ton against Afghanistan in 2013 .
