Sharad Pawar calls for joint meet The author has posted comments on this article Gaurav Gupta , TNN | Feb 19 , 2015 , 11.50AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : Ever since he recovered from a hip injury in January , Sharad Pawar has begun to take an active interest in cricket administration. The NCP supremo has attended the past two managing committee meetings of the Mumbai Cricket Association ( MCA ) , apart from chairing the Annual General Meeting ( AGM ) of the body. He has also announced that he will represent the MCA in the Board of Control for Cricket in India ( BCCI ) AGM on March 2 , where he may fight the presidential elections too . TOI has learnt that the former BCCI and ICC chief will , for the first time , chair a joint meeting of the re-launched Cricket Improvement Committee ( CIC ) and the Indoor cricket academy committee of the MCA at the BKC , on Saturday. " Saheb wants a better co-ordination between both the agencies. He wants to chart out a policy for the next one year for both , " said an MCA official . This is the first time that CIC and the MCA indoor academy will hold a joint meeting. Pawar himself formed the CIC when he took over as the MCA president in 2001 , in order to involve ex-cricketers in the cricketing affairs of the association .
