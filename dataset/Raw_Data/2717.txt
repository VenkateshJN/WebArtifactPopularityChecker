Chris Gayle will skip England for IPL The author has posted comments on this article Dwaipayan Datta , TNN | Mar 22 , 2015 , 10.30AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 WELLINGTON : After the New Zealand-West Indies quarterfinal got over , there was some speculation around the Westpac Stadium that Chris Gayle would announce his retirement from international cricket . But the maverick , who smashed 61 off 33 balls despite a back injury , is keen to carry on in all three formats . But it 's understood that he wo n't be going for the England series and will instead play the Indian Premier League for Royal Challengers Bangalore. " He is unlikely to go to England and instead will play IPL. It 's his decision and it 's unlikely that the West Indies Cricket Board will force him , " a source in the know of things said . His innings on Saturday was quite special and despite the disappointing loss , captain Jason Holder had a word of praise for him . " That 's how Chris is. He plays his own way and that 's how he was going even today. But I have no information about his retirement , it 's his decision and he will take a call , " Holder said . Meanwhile , Phil Simmons , who was in charge of Ireland till the World Cup , has been named the next West Indian coach .
