Badrinath to lead Vidarbha Twenty20 squad The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Mar 19 , 2015 , 03.31PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : The Vidarbha Cricket Association has declared the team for the Mushtaq Ali Twenty20 Zonal ( Central Zone ) Cricket tournament scheduled to be played at Indore from March 24 to 29 . S Badrinath will lead the side in the shortest format too , while Shalabh Shrivastava will be his deputy. Prolific scorer in the Guzder League T20 matches held earlier this month , Aniruddha Chore , failed to make the cut as he was ignored by the selectors . The Vidarbha team will leave for Indore in a day or two . Vidarbha team : S Badrinath ( captain ) , Shalabh Shrivastava ( vice-captain ) , Ganesh Satish , Jitesh Sharma , Ravi Jangid , Sumit Ruikar , Akshay Wakhare , Aditya Shanware , Shrikant Wagh , Amol Jungade , Yadvendra Tembhare , Apoorv Wankhade , Himanshu Joshi , Ravi Thakur , Urvesh Patel .
