( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 COLOMBO : South Africa pulled off a draw on the fifth and final day of the second Test against Sri Lanka on Monday to win the two-match series 1-0 . Chasing a target of 369 , South Africa finished on 159-8 off 111 overs. South Africa had won the opening Test in Galle by 153 runs .
