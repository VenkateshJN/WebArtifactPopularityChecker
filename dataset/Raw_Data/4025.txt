New gen rising - Buttler serves it hot The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 7 , 2015 , 11.55AM IST Page 1 of 4 Jos Buttler , 24 , wicketkeeper-batsman. 49 matches , 1139 runs , highest score 121 , strike rate 108.89 . England 's X-factor , a solid wicketkeeper and dynamic batsman , Buttler is developing a reputation as one of the most destructive batsmen in the game , with shots all-round the wicket . Buttler 's 121 against Sri Lanka last summer was the most eye-catching innings in recent times. As a lower-order batsman he has the talent to change the course of a game in the blink of the eye with his flamboyant strokeplay , although he 's still a work in progress behind the stumps . In the recently concluded tri-series , where England and India were engaged in a keen tussle to find a place in the final at Perth , Buttler brought energy to the proceedings and a level of calm when the Poms appeared to be caving in quickly at 6/65 . He got along with James Taylor to construct a 125-run stand for the sixth wicket which helped England cross the line. The World Cup offers a perfect stage for one of the game 's precocious talents .
