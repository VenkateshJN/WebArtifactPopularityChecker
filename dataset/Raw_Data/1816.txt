All-rounder Bisen shines in SBI win over BOI The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Mar 8 , 2015 , 11.39AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : State Bank of India ( SBI ) beat Bank of India ( BOI ) by 18 runs in the Bankers ' Sports Council-organized Inter-Bank 'A ' Division cricket tournament match here at the Vasant Nagar ground . Making first use of the pitch , SBI scored 116 for 5 in 20 overs. Vibhor Bisen top-scored with 40 ( 2x4 , 3x6 ) . Bisen was well supported by Nrupal Rode ( 25 ) and Sumit Sawatkar ( 22 not out ) . For BOI , Bharat Thakare and Vinod Gogate took two wickets each . BOI tried hard but were restricetd to 98 for 8. Sameer Gujar waged a lone battle with a fighting 62. Bisen and Sawatkar claimed two wickets apience , while Aditya Gokhale , Pratik Katarpawar and Nrupal Rode chipped in with a wicket each .
