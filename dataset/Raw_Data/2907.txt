( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 HOBART : Australia captain Michael Clarke won the toss and elected to field against Scotland in the World Cup Pool A match at Hobart 's Bellerive Oval on Saturday . Scorecard This was the last group match for both sides , with four-time champions and tournament co-hosts Australia already through to the quarterfinals , with the winless Scots no longer able to advance into the knockout stages . Australia will play an as yet to be decided opponent in a quarterfinal at the Adelaide Oval on March 20 . For this match , they recalled paceman Pat Cummins in place of spinner Xavier Doherty in the only change to the side that beat Sri Lanka by 64 runs in Sydney last week . Meanwhile Clarke was passed fit after missing training on Thursday to manage his longstanding hamstring injury .
