( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 NOTTINGHAM : Indian skipper Mahendra Singh Dhoni won the toss and invited England to bat first in the third one-day international at Trent Bridge on Saturday . The Indian think-tank has opted for Ambati Rayudu in place of injured Rohit Sharma , who fractured his middle finger of the right hand . For England , medium-pacer Steven Finn comes in place of Chris Jordan in the XI . Ajinkya Rahane is slated to open the innings alongwith Shikhar Dhawan . The first ODI was washed out in Bristol while India humbled the hosts in the second match to take 1-0 lead in the five-match series . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Shikhar Dhawan , Ambati Rayudu , Virat Kohli , Ajinkya Rahane , Suresh Raina , Ravindra Jadeja , Ravichandran Ashwin , Bhuvneshwar Kumar , Mohammed Shami and Mohit Sharma . England : Alastair Cook ( Capt. ) , Alex Hales , Ian Bell , Joe Root , Eoin Morgan , Jos Buttler , Ben Stokes , Chris Woakes , Steven Finn , James Tredwell and James Anderson .
