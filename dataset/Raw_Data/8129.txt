Aaron Finch Aaron Finch has been a star in limited-overs cricket for Australia , Victoria and Melbourne Renegades. Read More The opener 's greatest one-day international performance to date came in 2013 when he smashed 16 fours and seven sixes on his way to 148 from 114 balls against Scotland in 2013 . With four one-day centuries and three fifties in 2014 , including 109 against South Africa in Canberra in November , Finch is in good form for the World Cup . His performances in that series saw him achieve a career high ICC ODI ranking , surging 28 places to No.15 after compiling 250 runs at an average of 50 . The aggressive batsman from Colac will play in his first ICC Cricket World Cup in 2015 .
