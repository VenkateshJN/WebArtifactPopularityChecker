I love driving in and around CP after 10pm : Laxmi Ratan Shukla The author has posted comments on this article Pratyush Patra , TNN | Jan 30 , 2015 , 11.01PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 Cricketer Laxmi Ratan Shukla , who has represented the Delhi Daredevils in the IPL , was recently spotted at an event in the capital. When we spoke to the Bengali all-rounder about his Delhi experience , he told us that the only place he is acquainted with in Delhi is the Feroz Shah Kotla stadium . Talking about how he visits the Hanuman Mandir in CP whenever he is in NCR , he said , " I 'm a complete Hanuman bhakt and I like to spend time at the temple. Also , whenever I 'm in town , I love going out for drives with my friends in and around CP after 10pm , as there is not much traffic.
