Discover Australia ’ s stories in its national capital , Canberra . While this young , modern city – which turned 100 in 2013 – is most commonly known as the federal seat of government , it also has an array of national attractions that hold and share the essence of Australia . You ’ ll find experiences showcasing Australia ’ s history , culture and lifestyle including the Australian Institute of Sport , the National Museum of Australia , the National Gallery of Australia and the Australian War Memorial . The city , with a population of 380,000 , is designed around Lake Burley Griffin , a man-made lake popular with cyclists , walkers and joggers , and is an easy 2.5 hour drive to both the New South Wales South Coast and Snowy Mountains .
