World Cup 2015 - Final Squads ( Full Coverage ) Ajantha Mendis , Dilruwan Perera dropped ; Senanayake included in SL WC squad 2015/Jan/07 05 : 36 : 00 Text Size © AFP Sri Lanka have named their fifteen-man squad for the ICC Cricket World Cup 2015. Six players ( Kusal Perera , Ajantha Mendis , Thilana Kandamby , Lahiru Gamage , Shaminda Eranga , Dilruwan Perera ) from the side that played against England at home recently have been dropped. The squad named is identical to the one touring New Zealand for seven ODIs , with the exception of Shaminda Eranga , who has been named 16th man for the ODI series . The squad is filled to the brim with experience with the expected inclusions of Mahela Jayawardene , Kumar Sangakkara , Tillakaratne Dilshan , Lasith Malinga , Angelo Mathews and Nuwan Kulasekara , all of who played important roles in their run-up to the final defeat to India in 2011 . Malinga 's availability , however , is subject to the pacer proving fitness ahead of the World Cup , according to reports .
