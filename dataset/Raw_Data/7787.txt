30 October 201410 : 06 Australia Trophy Tour : Schedule The Australia leg of the ICC Cricket World Cup Trophy Tour begins on 10 November in Geraldton , WA , and ends in Sydney on Australia Day , January 26 , 2015. An open topped double decker bus will take the trophy to 65 locations where locals can get their own World Cup picture , test their cricketing skills and maybe even meet a World Cup legend .
