Brad Haddin Replacing Adam Gilchrist was never going to be an easy task for Brad Haddin , but the Canberra product has served his country with aplomb for almost 15 years. Read More The veteran has more than 100 ODI caps to his name and has scored two centuries and 16 fifties . The 37-year-old veteran scored 224 runs in 11 matches at an average of 32 in 2014 , with his best knock of 49 from 66 balls coming against Zimbabwe in August . This will be his second ICC Cricket World Cup appearance . At the 2011 tournament he had a top score of 88 against Canada .
