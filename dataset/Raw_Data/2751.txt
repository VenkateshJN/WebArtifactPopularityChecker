Hedaoo steers President XI to Joglekar Trophy triumph The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Feb 20 , 2015 , 12.52PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Former Vidarbha Ranji Trophy batsman Parimal Hedaoo slammed an unbeaten fifty to steer President XI to triumph in the VM Joglekar Trophy Twenty20 Cricket match , organized by Bankers ' Sports Council , at the Vasant Nagar ground . In a one-sided final , President XI defeated Secretary XI by 8 wickets . Opting to bat , Secretary XI were restricted to 75 for 8 in 20 overs. Girish Vitalkar remained unbeaten on 22 . Sanket Asre ( 14 ) and Ajay YAdav ( 12 ) were the other scorers . For President XI , Aditya Gokhlae scalped two wickets. Vibhor Bisen , Manish Dosi , Vinod Gogte and Milind Make took one wicket each . Man-of-the-match Hedaoo remained undefeated on 52 to help his side romp home easily. Dosi contributed 13 runs .
