( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 LONDON : England captain Alastair Cook won the toss and elected to bowl against India under overcast conditions in the fifth and final cricket Test at the Oval on Friday . India have made two changes to the side , bringing back pacer Ishant Sharma in place of Pankaj Singh while Stuart Binny takes place of Ravindra Jadeja . England are unchanged. The toss was delayed by 30 minutes due to wet outfield . India are trailing the series 1-2 , having won at Lord 's and lost at Southampton and Manchester . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Gautam Gambhir , Murali Vijay , Cheteshwar Pujara , Virat Kohli , Ajinkya Rahane , Stuart Binny , Ravichandran Ashwin , Ishant Sharma , Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Varun Aaron . England : Alastair Cook ( Capt. ) , Sam Robson , Gary Ballance , Moeen Ali , James Anderson , Ian Bell , Stuart Broad , Jos Buttler , Chris Jordan , Joe Root and Chris Woakes .
