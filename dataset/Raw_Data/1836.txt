Up to MCA to decide on my continuation as Mumbai coach : Amre The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 28 , 2015 , 04.17PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : Mumbai cricket coach Pravin Amre 's year-long tenure is coming to an end in three days but there is suspense over his continuation in the post . While a senior official of Mumbai Cricket Association ( MCA ) said Amre did not want an extension of his contract , the former cricketer said the ball was in MCA 's court . " My tenure is coming to an end on March 31. Now it 's upto them ( MCA ) to decide ( whether to retain him as coach ) , " Amre said on Saturday . However , MCA Joint secretary Nitin Dalal said Amre had given indication he did not want to continue as coach . " His tenure is till March 31 and he does not want to continue. Our CIC ( Cricket Improvement Committee headed by Madhav Apte ) will decide the future coach of all age groups teams , " said Dalal . Under Amre ( 46 ) , the team reached the semifinal of the Ranji Trophy before losing to eventual champions Karnataka . Mumbai have now made it to the knock out rounds of the Syed Mushtaq Ali T20 championship to be held in Cuttack from April 1-7 and Amre wo n't accompany the team as coach , Dalal said .
