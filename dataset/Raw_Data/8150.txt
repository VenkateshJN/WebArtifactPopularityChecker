Ross Taylor This talented batsman broke into the Black Caps One-Day International side in 2006 against West Indies. Since then , Taylor has carved himself a reputation as a prolific batsman across all forms of the game for New Zealand. Read More Taylor was part of the Black Caps teams in the 2007 and 2011 ICC Cricket World Cups . His highest ODI score is 131 * , scored against Pakistan in the World cup in 2011 .
