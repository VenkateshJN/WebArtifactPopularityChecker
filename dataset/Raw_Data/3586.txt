England beat India to win lone T20 The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Sep 7 , 2014 , 10.58PM IST England 's Eoin Morgan hits out during the international T20 cricket match against India at Edgbaston. ( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 BIRMINGHAM : England beat India by three runs to win the lone Twenty20 international at Edgbaston on Sunday . India , chasing 181 to win , finished on 177 for five. They needed 17 off the last over and that became five -- effectively six -- off the final ball , or a four to send the match into a 'super-over' . But India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni , who finished on 27 not out , was unable to hit the required boundary off paceman Chris Woakes . Earlier , England captain Eoin Morgan made 71 off just 31 balls , including three fours and seven sixes in a total of 180 for seven after the former Ireland international won the toss .
