Steven Smith slams 4th ODI century in World Cup semifinal The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 26 , 2015 , 12.18PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 SYDNEY : Australia 's Steven Smith blasted an 89-ball century to give his team the upper hand in the World Cup semifinal against India at the Sydney Cricket Ground on Thursday . Smith , batting at No.3 , raced through the nineties with a six and four off successive balls to claim his fourth ODI century and first at the World Cup . Smith looked in tremendous touch and hit 10 fours and two sixes as he and Aaron Finch put on 173 in an unbroken second wicket partnership .
