MPs to croon in support of Dhoni and Co The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 20 , 2015 , 09.31PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : A " spontaneous " humming of few lines by BJP MP 's Manoj Tiwari and Anurag Thakur on Mahendra Singh Dhoni at the Parliament 's Central Hall is going to take the shape of a full-fledged song before India take on Australia in the World Cup semifinal on March 26 . The light-hearted moment was uploaded by Thakur on Youtube where NCP MP Supriya Sule and BJP MP Meenakshi Lekhi can also be seen crooning " Hone wala anhoni hai , jeet ke aane wala Dhoni hai " . " The lines came to me spontaneously but later I added lyrics to the song. I will compose music for it and make it public soon , " Tiwari said . " I appreciate Supriyaji , Meenakshiji , Anuragji for joining in. That was such a sporting gesture on their part. Supriya even recorded the whole moment , " he added . Showering effusive praise on the Indian cricket captain , Tiwari , BJP 's North East Delhi MP , said he admired Dhoni as he thinks was " not only a good cricketer but also a good human being " . " I believe that when I sing for him I sing for the whole team. I wish them all the success , " he said . On being asked whether he was planning a quick dash to Australia for the semifinal encounter against Pakistan he said , " I have certain superstitions in this regard so I would watch the match sitting at home as usual... do n't want to take any chances , " he smiled .
