( TOI Photo ) Page 1 of 4 BANGALORE : Royal Challengers Bangalore captain Virat Kohli won the toss and opted to bowl in their IPL match against Kings XI Punjab on Friday . RCB made two changes with Sachin Rana and Albie Morkel coming in place of Rilee Roussow and Ashok Dinda . For Kings XI Punjab , Lakshmipathy Balaji is back after sitting out the last match and Shivam Sharma is making his IPL debut. They replace Rishi Dhawan and Murali Kartik . Teams : Kings XI Punjab : George Bailey ( c ) , Mandeep Singh , Virender Sehwag , Wriddhiman Saha ( wk ) , Glenn Maxwell , David Miller , Akshar Patel , Mitchell Johnson , Shivam Sharma , Lakshmipathy Balaji , Sandeep Sharma . Royal Challengers Bangalore : Virat Kohli ( c ) , Chris Gayle , Parthiv Patel ( wk ) , AB de Villiers , Yuvraj Singh , Albie Morkel , Mitchell Starc , Sachin Rana , Harshal Patel , Varun Aaron , Yuzvendra Chahal .
