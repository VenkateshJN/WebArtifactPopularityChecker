World Cup moments - Stevie wonder after Gibbs ' blunder The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Jan 30 , 2015 , 01.46PM IST Page 1 of 4 1999 AUSTRALIA VS SOUTH AFRICA , LEEDS Herschelle Gibbs ' early celebration of the Steve Waugh catch and the subsequent spill is part of Cup folklore . But let 's not take anything away from Steve 's knock. Chasing 272 to win the Super Six game , the Aussie captain scored an unbeaten 120 ( 110b , 10x4s , 2x6s ) ) and helped his team win by five wickets with two balls to spare . The knock was downright ugly but mighty effective : the slog sweep , lofted off-drive , hoick over square-leg and semi-intentional shots over the fielders . On one occasion , Steve fell on the ground in his follow through as the ball sped past the boundary ! If he was the difference between victory and defeat , the win in the match was also about a spot in the final . For , when the same teams played a nail-biting tie in the semis , Australia advanced to the final because they finished ahead of SA after the Super Six stage. This win made it possible .
