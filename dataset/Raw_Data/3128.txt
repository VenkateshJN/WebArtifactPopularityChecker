World Cup 2015 : Statistical highlights of India-UAE match The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Feb 28 , 2015 , 08.55PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 PERTH : Statistical highlights of the pool B match between India and UAE at the ICC World Cup 2015 here on Saturday . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 # UAE ( 102 ) have posted their lowest World Cup total , surpassing the 136 vs England at Peshawar on February 18 , 1996 . # UAE 's aforesaid score is actually their lowest in ODIs - the previous lowest was 123 vs Sri Lanka at Dambulla on July 17 , 2004 . # The above total is the lowest by any team against India in World Cups , outstripping the 109 by Sri Lanka at Johannesburg on March 10 , 2003 . # UAE 's 102 is the tenth lowest score by any team against India in ODIs . # Shaiman Anwar ( 35 ) has finished as the highest scorer in UAE 's innings. With 208 runs at an average of 69.33 , including a century and a fifty , in three innings , he is the leading run-getter for UAE batsman in World Cups . # Ravindra Jadeja ( 2/23 ) has recorded his best bowling performance in World Cup . # Umesh Yadav ( 2/15 ) has recorded his best bowling figures in World Cup , bettering the 2 for 50 vs Pakistan at Adelaide on Feb.15 , 2015 . # India have won all three World Cup matches by big margins in the present edition - by 76 runs vs Pakistan at Adelaide on Feb.15 , by 130 runs vs South Africa at Melbourne on Feb.22 and by 9 wickets vs UAE at Perth on Feb.28 . # India have outclassed UAE in all three ODIs - by 71 runs at Sharjah on April 13 , 1994 ; by 116 runs at Dambulla on July 16 , 2004 and by 9 wickets at Perth on February 28 , 2015 . # India 's magnificent nine-wicket win is their first World Cup victory at Perth. England had defeated India by 9 runs on Feb. 22 , 1992 in the only World Cup game previously played by India at Perth . # Ravichandran Ashwin ( 4/25 ) has recorded his first four-wicket haul in ODIs , eclipsing the 3 for 24 vs New Zealand at Chennai on December 10 , 2010 . # Ashwin 's impressive figures are the third best by an Indian bowler in ODIs at Perth. Ravi Shastri had captured five for 15 vs Australia on December 8 , 1991 and Irfan Pathan - four for 24 vs Zimbabwe on February 3 , 2004 . # Ashwin 's superb figures are the third best by an Indian spinner in World Cups. Yuvraj Singh is the only Indian spinner to capture five wickets in an innings - five for 31 vs Ireland at Bangalore on March 6 , 2011 , followed by four for six vs Namibia at Pietermaritzburg on February 23 , 2003 . # Ashwin 's figures are the best by an Indian bowler in ODIs vs UAE , bettering Sachin Tendulkar 's figures of three for 21 at Dambulla on July 16 , 2004 . # Ashwin has got the man of the match award for the first time in World Cups - his third in ODIs . # Nine times , the tenth-wicket pair has recorded the highest partnership in an ODI innings against India - the most against any team .
