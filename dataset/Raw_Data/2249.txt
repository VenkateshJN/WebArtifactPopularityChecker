( TOI Photo ) RELATED RANCHI : Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to bat against Chennai Super Kings in an IPL match at the JSCA International stadium on Tuesday . CSK have dropped Mithun Manhas to give IPL debut to batsman Vijay Shankar. RR have made three changes to their playing eleven . They dropped Sanju Samson , Kane Richardson and Rahul Tewatia , brought in wicketkeeper Dishant Yagnik , Trinidad & Tobago all-rounder Kevon Cooper and left-arm spinner Ankit Sharma .
