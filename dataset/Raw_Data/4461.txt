Windies feel the Curtly Ambrose effect The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Oct 11 , 2014 , 12.29PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : The inimitable Curtly Ambrose , who was hired as West Indies bowling coach back in May this year , is working hard on injecting the team with some " positive energy " , according to skipper Dwayne Bravo . With yet another crisis looming that involves the West Indies Players ' Association , the team seems to be working extra hard on keeping motivation levels up . The nets session was preceded by an intense and lengthy team interaction , with the legendary Ambrose standing tall over the proceedings . " Curtly adds a different dimension to the team. These are players I have grown up watching and we are lucky to have them around and get some advice , " said Bravo .
