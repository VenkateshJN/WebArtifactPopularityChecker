Bangladesh are without regular captain Mashrafe Mortaza who is suffering from a sore throat and a calf injury . Shakib Al Hasan leads the team in Mortaza 's absence . As expected , Adam Milne was left out with a shoulder problem and was replaced with left-arm paceman Mitchell McClenaghan in the first change for New Zealand in the tournament . Teams : New Zealand : Brendon McCullum ( Capt. ) , Martin Guptill , Kane Williamson , Ross Taylor , Grant Elliott , Mitchell McClenaghan , Daniel Vettori , Corey Anderson , Tim Southee , Luke Ronchi , Trent Boult . Bangladesh : Shakib Al Hasan ( Capt. ) , Tamim Iqbal , Imrul Kayes , Mushfiqur Rahim , Mohammad Mahmudullah , Nasir Hossain , Sabbir Rahman , Soumya Sarkar , Rubel Hossain , Taskin Ahmed , Taijul Islam . Umpires : Richard Kettleborough ( ENG ) and Kumar Dharmasena ( SRI ) . TV umpire : Nigel Llong ( ENG ) . Match referee : Chris Broad ( ENG ) .
