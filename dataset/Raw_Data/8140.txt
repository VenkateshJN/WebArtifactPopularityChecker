Pat Cummins Having burst onto the scene as a 17-year-old in 2010-11 , Pat Cummins was quickly identified as a future star and became the youngest player ever to earn a central contract with Cricket Australia. Read More Despite having only a handful of games to his name , he was given his first one-day international cap against South Africa in October 2011 . The fast bowler ’ s best ODI figures to date came in his first match and he was included in the Australia team for the November 2014 ODI series against South Africa . The paceman took 2-61 and 3-54 in two outings during that series . The ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 is Cummins ’ first appearance at the tournament .
