World Cup 2015 : South Africa put Pakistan into bat The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 7 , 2015 , 08.56AM IST South Africa have won three of their four matches. ( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 AUCKLAND : South African captain AB de Villiers won the toss and sent Pakistan in to bat in the World Cup Pool B match at Eden Park in Auckland on Saturday . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 South Africa have won three of their four matches and another win will ensure their place in the quarterfinals along with defending champions India who earned a berth in the next round with a win over the West Indies on Friday . Fit-again JP Duminy returned for the Proteas in place of Farhan Behardien . Pakistan , who have two wins in four matches , dropped struggling opener Nasir Jamshed while Haris Sohail was ruled out with a heel injury. In came , Younis Khan and Sarfraz Ahmed . Teams : Pakistan : Misbah-ul-Haq ( Capt. ) , Ahmed Shehzad , Sarfraz Ahmed , Younis Khan , Umar Akmal , Sohaib Maqsood , Shahid Afridi , Wahab Riaz , Rahat Ali , Sohail Khan , Mohammad Irfan . South Africa : AB de Villiers ( Capt. ) , Hashim Amla , Quinton de Kock , Faf du Plessis , Rilee Rossouw , David Miller , JP Duminy , Kyle Abbott , Dale Steyn , Morne Morkel , Imran Tahir . Umpires : Richard Kettleborough ( ENG ) and Bruce Oxenford ( AUS ) . TV umpire : Chris Gaffaney ( NZL ) Match referee : Chris Broad .
