( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 MELBOURNE : Australian batsman Shaun Marsh on Tuesday became only the 16th player in the history of Test cricket to be run out on 99 . Marsh , attempting a risky single , was run out at the non-striker 's end by Virat Kohli throw on the fifth and final day in the ongoing third Test against India . He became the third Australian to have this unwanted record against his name after Bill Brown ( 1948 ) and Arthur Morris ( 1953 ) .
