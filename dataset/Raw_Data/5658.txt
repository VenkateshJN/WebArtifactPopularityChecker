Irfan ruled out of World Cup due to injury Adelaide , Tue , Mar 17 2015 Last updated on Tuesday , 17 March , 2015 , 07 : 28 AM Mohammad Irfan will miss the World Cup due to a hip injury . Irfan missed the clash against Ireland due to the injury . Mohammad Irfan has been ruled out of the World Cup due to a hip injury. © Getty Pakistan left-arm fast bowler Mohammad Irfan has been ruled out of the World Cup due to a stress fracture in his hip. The PCB has said that a replacement will be flown in only if Pakistan win the quarter-final against Australia in Adelaide on Friday . Earlier , the PCB had released a statement saying that Irfan had gone for MRI scans in Adelaide after experiencing pain in his right hip. Speaking to Reuters , one PCB official said initial reports of Irfan were not encouraging and he could miss the rest of the World Cup due to the injury . Irfan has had a modest 2015 ICC Cricket World Cup with eight wickets in five games at an average of 23.25. His best performances came against Zimbabwe in Brisbane where his haul of 4 for 30 helped Pakistan win a close match while he put in a good show against South Africa in Auckland by picking up 3 for 52 . Irfan was replaced by Ehsan Adil in Pakistan 's last league game against Ireland in Adelaide and he ended up with figures of 7-0-31-1 . © Cricbuzz ( with inputs from agencies ) Tags
