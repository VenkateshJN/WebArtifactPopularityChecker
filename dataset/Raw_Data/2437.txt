( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 LEEDS : Indian captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni won the toss and elected to bowl in the fifth and final cricket one-dayer against England at Headingley in Leeds on Friday . India have made one change to the squad , bringing in pacer Umesh Yadav to replace Dhawal Kulkarni . England have made two changes with James Tredwell and Ben Stokes replacing Harry Gurney and Gary Ballance . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Shikhar Dhawan , Ajinkya Rahane , Virat Kohli , Ambati Rayudu , Suresh Raina , Ravindra Jadeja , R Ashwin , Bhuvneshwar Kumar , Mohammed Shami , Umesh Yadav . England : Alastair Cook ( Capt. ) , Alex Hales , Moeen Ali , Joe Root , Eoin Morgan , Jos Buttler , Ben Stokes , Chris Woakes , James Tredwell , Steve Finn , James Anderson .
