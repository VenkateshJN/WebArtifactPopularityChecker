All-rounder Gohe leads RPF to cricket title The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Mar 2 , 2015 , 11.38AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Railway Protection Force ( RPF ) reigned supreme in the Inter-Department cricket tournament , organized by the South Eastern Central Railway Sports Association , recently here at the Motibagh ground . Fourteen departmental teams took part in the annual tourney. In the summit clash , RPF trounced Engineering department by five wickets . Opting to bat , Engineering department were restricted to 94-8 in 12 overs. Durgesh Yadav ( 39 ) and Duryesh Bhonge ( 10 ) were the notable scorers. Amit Gohe scalped two wickets , while Mohanlal , Singh , Kulmate and Gupta took one wicket each . RPF struggled but reached target with just one ball and 5 wickets to spare. Amit Gohe ( 30 ) , Dable ( 18 ) and Dubey ( 14 ) batted well. Sachin Raghavate , Sanjay Kale , Raju Pardeshi bagged a wicket each for the losers .
