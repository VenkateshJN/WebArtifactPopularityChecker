India vs Australia 2nd Test : Australia chase 128 for victory against India The author has posted comments on this article Reuters | Dec 20 , 2014 , 09.49AM IST Australian team celebrate the dismissal of Virat Kohli on the fourth day of the 2nd Test at Gabba in Brisbane. ( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 Australia will chase 128 for victory and an unassailable 2-0 series lead after dismissing India for 224 before tea on the fourth day of the second Test at the Gabba in Brisbane on Saturday . A magical early spell from Mitchell Johnson ( 4-61 ) blew a tight Test match wide open in the first 20 minutes and although injured opener Shikhar Dhawan ( 81 ) provided some resistance after lunch , the hosts mopped up the tail midway through the afternoon . India had resumed on 71/1 without opener Dhawan , who was injured warming up in the nets , looking to rub the last 26 runs off their first innings deficit and build another imposing tally to match their first innings 408 . Johnson , though , reprised the onslaught that drove his country to victory in the opening Ashes Test at the same ground last year , taking three for 10 in 11 balls .
