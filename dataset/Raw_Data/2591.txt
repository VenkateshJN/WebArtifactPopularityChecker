Sri Lanka beat England by seven runs in 4th ODI The author has posted comments on this article AFP | May 31 , 2014 , 11.19PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 LONDON : Sri Lanka beat England by seven runs to win the fourth one-day international at Lord 's which levelled the five-match series at 2-2 . Just days after collapsing to 67 all out in a 10-wicket loss in the third ODI at Old Trafford , Sri Lanka made 300 for nine with Kumar Sangakkara top-scoring with 112 . England slumped to 111 for five in reply before Jos Buttler 's brilliant 121 , his maiden ODI hundred , gave the hosts hope of an improbable win before they fell just short .
