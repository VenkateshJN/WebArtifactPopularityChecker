( TOI Photo ) RELATED HYDERABAD : Kings XI Punjab captain George Bailey won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in their IPL match at the Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium on Wednesday . Sunrisers Hyderabad will have to quickly put their seven-wicket defeat against Mumbai Indians behind them for their game against Kings XI Punjab , who started IPL 7 in a rousing manner and continued the good work till Knight Riders Kolkata halted their run in Cuttack . Now stuck on eight points from nine games , the Sunrisers need to address the problem areas that seem to bog them down in the middle overs if they intend to make a match of it against Punjab , one of the two top teams in this edition along with Chennai Super Kings . The toast of the tournament is Maxwell , the 25-year-old Victorian topping the tournament batting charts with 474 runs from nine innings .
