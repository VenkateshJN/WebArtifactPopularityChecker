Yuvraj Singh confident of India retaining World Cup The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 16 , 2015 , 06.34PM IST Yuvraj Singh opines that MS Dhoni 's strategies have worked well against the big teams in the 50-over showpiece event. ( TOI Photo ) Page 1 of 4 GWALIOR : Praising Mahendra Singh Dhoni 's efficient leadership , out-of-favour Indian all-rounder Yuvraj Singh said that the team 's spectacular showing in this edition so far makes him believe that the Men in Blue can retain the trophy . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 " The way the Indian cricket is performing under the captaincy of Mahendra Singh Dhoni in the World Cup clearly manifests that it will retain the world champions title , " Yuvraj , who inaugurated a cricket academy at ITM University here , told reporters . The left-handed batsman also opines that Dhoni 's strategies have worked well against the big teams in the 50-over showpiece event . Yuvraj recalled the days of playing against Pakistan speedster Shoaib Akhtar alongwith captain Sourav Ganguly in Karachi . " His balling speed was over 150 km which was a challenge , " the all-rounder recounted . Yuvraj also said in a lighter vein that Akhtar is a good commentator as well .
