( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : The BCCI has finally decided to give the overworked stars of Indian cricket a much-needed break . The 15-member squad announced on Wednesday for the three-match ODI series against Bangladesh will be led by Suresh Raina instead of MS Dhoni , who along with the other regulars , Virat Kohli , Rohit Sharma , Shikhar Dhawan , R Ashwin , Ravindra Jadeja , Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Mohammed Shami have been rested ahead of the tour of England . The Indian team has virtually been on the road since last November , playing in South Africa , New Zealand followed by the Asia Cup , ICC World T20 and now the IPL . The series , which gets underway on June 15 , will also see the return of opener Robin Uthappa , who had last played ODIs in 2008 . The Karnataka player was selected on the basis of his good form in the IPL where he is currently the highest run-getter . Raina too returns to the ODI squad after being ignored for the Asia Cup following a run of poor scores . The other notable inclusion is Maharashtra 's Kedar Jadhav , who with 1223 runs was the highest scorer in the Ranji Trophy this season. The squad also has Gujarat all-rounder Akshar Patel who has impressed with his temperament in IPL 7 . Squad for Bangladesh tour : Suresh Raina ( Capt. ) , Robin Uthappa , Ajinkya Rahane , Cheteshwar Pujara , Ambati Rayudu , Manoj Tiwary , Kedar Jadhav , Wriddhimaan Saha , Parveez Rasool , Akshar Patel , Vinay Kumar , Umesh Yadav , Stuart Binny , Mohit Sharma , Amit Mishra .
