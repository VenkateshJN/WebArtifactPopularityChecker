( PTI Photo ) RELATED NEW DELHI : Chennai Super Kings skipper MS Dhoni won the toss and elected to bowl against Delhi Daredevils in their IPL match at Feroz Shah Kotla Stadium in Delhi on Monday . Super Kings are playing an unchanged side , while the Daredevils have made one change , bringing in Laxmi Ratan Shukla in place of Rahul Sharma .
