Union Bank emerge 'B ' division cricket champions The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Mar 15 , 2015 , 01.10PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Union Bank of India thrashed Axis Bank by nine wickets to win the Inter-Bank 'B ' Division Cricket tournament title for the second time here recently. The annual tourney was organized by the Bankers ' Sports Council . Put in to bat , Axis Bank were bowled out for mere 109 in 18 overs. Abhishek Choudhary was the top scorer with 32 , while Vikrant Ghomade contributed 29. For Union Bank , Vishal Sontakke and Rajesh Gawande pocketed two wickets each . Union Bank romped home by losing a solitary wicket of Nitin Gupta ( 28 ) . Vaibhav Dharme and Ajay Yadav remained unbeaten on 41 and 28 , respectively .
