World Cup 2015 : Afghanistan elect to bowl against Australia The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 4 , 2015 , 11.59AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 PERTH ( Australia ) : Afghanistan captain Mohammad Nabi won the toss and decided to bowl in the World Cup Pool B game against Australia at the WACA Ground on Wednesday . Australia made two changes for the match , with all-rounder Shane Watson left out , along with paceman Pat Cummins . All-rounder James Faulkner was recalled after injury , with Josh Hazlewood also back into the team . Afghanistan promoted opener Usman Ghani at the expense of Gulbadin Naib . Teams : Australia : Michael Clarke ( capt ) , Aaron Finch , David Warner , Steve Smith , Glenn Maxwell , Mitchell Marsh , Brad Haddin , James Faulkner , Mitchell Johnson , Mitchell Starc , Josh Hazlewood . Afghanistan : Mohammad Nabi ( capt ) , Javed Ahmadi , Usman Ghani , Nawroz Mangal , Asghar Stanikzai , Samiullah Shinwari , Najib Zadran , Afsar Kkhan Zazai , Dawlat Zadran , Hamid Hassan , Shahpur Zadran .
