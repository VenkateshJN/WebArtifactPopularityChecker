World Cup 2015 : India-Australia semifinal generates 24.5 million interactions on Facebook The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Mar 27 , 2015 , 01.56PM IST Umesh Yadav and Mohammed Shami walk off the field after Australia won their World Cup semifinal match in Sydney. ( Reuters Photo ) Page 1 of 4 As Australia and India faced off on Thursday to compete for a spot in the ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 final , conversation on Facebook exploded as millions joined in ball-by-ball commentary during the crucial game . Full Coverage : ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 Globally , 8.8 million people drove 24.5 million interactions ( posts , comments and likes ) around the match on Facebook. In India , 6 million people had 17 million interactions . In addition to the volume of global conversation , Facebook 's data team looked at the top demographics and most talked-about players on Facebook during the match . For Team India , Virat Kohli dominated the conversation , while Steve Smith was the most buzzed about Australian player . Though men dominated the number of people using the Facebook to share the match experience , women are not far behind.
