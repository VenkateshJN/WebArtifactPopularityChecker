( TOI Photo ) RELATED CUTTACK : Having finally achieved the much needed consistency , the Kolkata Knight Riders are a much relaxed outfit and opener Robin Uthappa a satisfied man . With his partnership with skipper Gautam Gambhir at the top of the order bearing rich dividends for the side , Uthappa is happy that the side is finally playing to its potential . " It 's good to win back to back matches. The bowlers have been consistent but it 's good that the batsmen are finally coming good , " he said after KKR 's Sunday win . With Gambhir bagging the man of the match award , Uthappa said that it was very important for the skipper to be back amongst the runs . " His return to form was very vital. Our partnership has been good at the top. The batting bears a settled look now and that bodes well for the side , " he said . Kings XI Punjab , meanwhile , are looking to take the positives and move on . " It 's a bad loss but we would like to take the positives out of it and move on to the next game.
