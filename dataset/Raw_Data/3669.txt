Hockey team sends best wishes to Indian cricketers The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Feb 12 , 2015 , 09.01PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MUMBAI : Indian hockey captain Sardar Singh on Thursday wished the national cricket team the very best in the World Cup beginning in Australia and New Zealand from Saturday . " As the most coveted cricket tournament- the ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 is all set to kick off , I and the entire Indian hockey team would like to extend our heartiest wishes to the boys in blue , " Sardar said . " I hope they play in the best spirit of the game and fight hard to bring back the World Cup. I believe this team has the potential to defend its title and bring joy to a billion hearts. I wish the Indian team the very best of luck for the World Cup , " he said in a statement . Defending champions India play their opening match against Pakistan at the Adelaide Oval on February 15 .
