RCB win toss , invite Hyderabad to bat first PTI | May 4 , 2014 , 07.49 PM IST Royal Challengers Bangalore skipper Virat Kohli elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad. ( TOI Photo ) RELATED BANGALORE : Royal Challengers Bangalore skipper Virat Kohli won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in an IPL match on Sunday . Hyderabad have brought back paceman Ishant Sharma , who replaced spinner Amit Mishra . The hosts have made two changes bringing in Rilee Rossouw and Harshal Patel for Albie Morkel and Yogesh Takawale .
