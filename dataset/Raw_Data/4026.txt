New gen rising - the quick pick The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 8 , 2015 , 12.29PM IST Page 1 of 4 PAT CUMMINS , 21 , PACER , 10 MATCHES , 14 WKTS Few bowlers have made more of an impression on their Test debut than Cummins did as a teenager with his 6-79 to lead Australia to a two-wicket victory over South Africa at Wanderers in November 2011 . At 18 , he became the second youngest man in history to take six wickets in an innings and what 's more , he even helped his side level the series by hitting the winning runs , besides taking seven wickets in the match . While the last three years have been mainly spent recovering from back and foot injuries , his body has held up well enough this season for Australia to gamble on selecting him in their youthful pace attack . Twenty-one years of age certainly is n't too old for Cummins to reclaim his mantle of " next big thing " , as he prepares to unleash against some of the best sides . The Aussie pace arsenal is perhaps currently the best in the world and a fit and relatively fresh Cummins , with his ability to mix his pace cleverly and bowl yorkers and slower-bouncers at will , gives the hosts a more than viable option .
