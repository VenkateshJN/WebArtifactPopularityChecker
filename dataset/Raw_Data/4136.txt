Rain washes out 3rd Ind-Ban ODI , India pocket series 2-0 The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Jun 19 , 2014 , 08.02PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MIRPUR : The third ODI between India and Bangladesh has been called off after heavy showers rendered the ground unfit for play , on Thursday . India were 119 for nine in 34.2 overs when rain stopped play for the third time in the final ODI of the series . Stuart Binny ( 25 ) and last man Umesh Yadav ( 0 ) were at the crease when heavens opened up again at the Shere Bangla National Stadium . India skipper Suresh Raina had won the toss and elected to bat first . India , thus , pocketed the three-match series 2-0 after having won the first two ODIs of the series . Brief Scores : India : 119 for nine in 34.2 overs ( Cheteshwar Pujara 27 , Stuart Binny 25 not out ; Shakib Al Hasan 3/27 ) .
