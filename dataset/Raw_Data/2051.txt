( AP Photo ) RELATED HYDERABAD : Sunrisers Hyderabad 's new skipper Darren Sammy won the toss and elected to bat against Kolkata Knight Riders in an IPL match , on Sunday . Hyderabad brought back Sammy , thrusting him into captaincy and dropped Moises Henriques while KKR are unchanged .
