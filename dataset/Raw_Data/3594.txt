England relish winner-take-all final clash The author has posted comments on this article Santosh Suri , TNN | Jan 28 , 2015 , 09.15AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 PERTH : England are relishing a do-or-die contest against India at the WACA on Friday , which would serve them well if they make the final against Australia on Sunday. England 's assistant coach , Paul Farbrace , was candid enough to say that India have been helped by the washout at Sydney and the two points they secured from the abandoned match against Australia have made things easier for them . " They ( India ) have gained a bit by taking two points. But it does n't change things for us , in a sense it 's what we had planned for this series , " said Farbrace. " To have a must-win game on Friday is just what the World Cup is going to be about. It 's a great opportunity for us to play two of the best teams in the world in the space of three days . " Effectively it 's a semifinal and a final. That 's what you want as often as possible at this level and that 's what we 're hoping to be involved in the latter part of the World Cup.
