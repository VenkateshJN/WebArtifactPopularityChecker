World Cup : Ahmedabad celebrates India 's big win The author has posted comments on this article Shweta Singh , TNN | Feb 15 , 2015 , 11.25PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 AHMEDABAD : It 's not just India 's Prime Minister Narendra Modi , who led a wave of congratulations for India 's cricket team as it opened its campaign in the World Cup with a resounding 76-run victory over rival Pakistan , who is in celebratory mode but the whole Ahmedabad city was seen rejoicing in the win . " Congrats Team India. Well played. We are all very proud of you , " Modi had said on his twitter account . Many Ahmedabad fans aired there emotions online when they put up celebratory pictures on various social networking site . Adarsh Jain said that he busted a cracker with every falling wicket of the opposition and made his friends to sit at one position all through Pakistan 's innings considering it as auspicious for Team India . Even fans had gathered at tea shops and in streets to watch the game in public went around the city with Indian flag flying high after the victory in Adelaide . However , one Krutika Desai made it a point that she did not see the match . " Whenever I watch India play they lose. I could not have seen India lose to Pakistan so I gave the match a miss and thankfully we won , " an elated Krutika said .
