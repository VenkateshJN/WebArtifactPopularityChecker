( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 MANCHESTER : Indian skipper Mahendra Singh Dhoni has won the toss and elected to bat first against England in the fourth Test at Old Trafford on Thursday . It is still overcast as the match has been delayed by half-an-hour due to morning rain in the city . The Indian think-tank has come up with three changes from the Southampton game , which the visitors lost by 266 runs as the five-match series is tied at 1-1 . Gautam Gammbhir finally gets a place in the XI in place of Shikhar Dhawan. Rohit Sharma makes way for Ravichandran Ashwin while Varun Aaron comes in place of Mohammed Shami . England captain Alastair Cook was happy to announce that the Three Lions have stuck to their winning combination . Teams : England : Alastair Cook ( c ) , Sam Robson , Gary Ballance , Ian Bell , Joe Root , Moeen Ali , Jos Buttler ( wk ) , Chris Woakes , Stuart Broad , James Anderson , Chris Jordan India : Murali Vijay , Gautam Gambhir , Cheteshwar Pujara , Virat Kohli , Ravichandran Ashwin , Ajinkya Rahane , Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( C and wk ) , Ravindra Jadeja , Bhuvneshwar Kumar , Varun Aaron , Pankaj Singh .
