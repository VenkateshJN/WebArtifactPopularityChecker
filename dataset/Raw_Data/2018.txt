Mumbai Indians invite Sunrisers Hyderabad to bat first PTI | Apr 30 , 2014 , 07.58 PM IST Mumbai Indians have dropped Australian Michael Hussey to include wicket-keeper batsman Ben Dunk in the playing eleven. ( Agency Photo ) RELATED DUBAI : Mumbai Indians skipper Rohit Sharma won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in an IPL match on Wednesday . Mumbai have dropped Australian Michael Hussey to include wicket-keeper batsman Ben Dunk . The Hyderabad side has made two changes , bringing in Irfan Pathan and Naman Ojha for Ishant Sharma and Y Venugopal Rao .
