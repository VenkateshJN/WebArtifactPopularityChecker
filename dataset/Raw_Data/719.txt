Â© AFP West Indies ace spinner Sunil Narine is likely to withdraw from the upcoming ICC World Cup in Australia and New Zealand starting on February 14 , according to media reports. It is believed that Narine 's decision has to do with continuous work on his bowling action . The 26-year-old off-spinner was reported for a suspect action while playing for the Indian Premier League giants Kolkata Knight Riders during the Champions League T20 ( CLT20 ) in September last year. He was later suspended from participation in BCCI organised tournaments . He was included in the West Indies 15-man squad led by Jason Holder for the World Cup . Kieron Pollard and Dwayne Bravo were left out for the quadrennial tournament .
