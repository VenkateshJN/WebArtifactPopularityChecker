World Cup 2015 : West Indies opt to bat against India The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 6 , 2015 , 12.03PM IST The West Indies have two wins in four matches in this World Cup. ( AP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 PERTH : West Indies captain Jason Holder won the toss and elected to bat in the World Cup Pool B game against India at the WACA Ground in Perth on Friday . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 India recalled seamer Mohammed Shami at the expense of Bhuvneshwar Kumar while the West Indies made one change , bringing in pace bowler Kemar Roach for spinner Sulieman Benn . India are unbeaten in the tournament with three wins in three games , while the West Indies have two wins in four matches . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Rohit Sharma , Shikhar Dhawan , Virat Kohli , Ajinkya Rahane , Suresh Raina , Ravindra Jadeja , Ravi Ashwin , Mohammed Shami , Mohit Sharma , Umesh Yadav . West Indies : Jason Holder ( Capt. ) , Chris Gayle , Dwayne Smith , Marlon Samuels , Jonathan Carter , Denesh Ramdin , Lendl Simmons , Darren Sammy , Andrew Russell , Jerome Taylor , Kemar Roach . Umpires : Kumar Dharmasena ( SRI ) and Nigel Llong ( ENG ) TV umpire : Billy Bowden ( NZL ) Match referee : Ranjan Madugalle ( SRI ) .
