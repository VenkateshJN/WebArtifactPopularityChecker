( TOI Photo ) RELATED ABU DHABI : Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in their Indian Premier League match at the Sheikh Zayed Stadium on Friday . Four debutants will play for Rajasthan , namely Abhishek Nayar , Kane Richardson , Rajat Bhatia and Dhawal Kulkarni , while Hyderabad are going with three fast bowlers -- Aaron Finch , David Warner , Dale Steyn .
