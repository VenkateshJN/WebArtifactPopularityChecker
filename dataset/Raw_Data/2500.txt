England make 167 against Scotland in rain-curtailed ODI The author has posted comments on this article AFP | May 9 , 2014 , 10.41PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 ABERDEEN : England made 167 for six against Scotland in the lone one-day international at Aberdeen on Friday after rain before and during their innings reduced the match to a 20 over per side contest . Ian Bell top-scored with 50 and England captain Alastair Cook made 44 after the visitors lost the toss . Somerset seamer Josh Davey led Scotland 's attack with three wickets for 28 runs , including two in two balls in the final over . Scotland were set an adjusted target of 173 to win under the Duckworth/Lewis method for rain-affected matches .
