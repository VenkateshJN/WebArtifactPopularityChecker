IPL 7 final will be in Mumbai , says MCA Gaurav Gupta Gaurav Gupta,TNN | May 8 , 2014 , 11.48 AM IST READ MORE Sunil Gavaskar | Ravi Savant | Mumbai Indians | Mumbai Cricket Association | IPL governing council RELATED MUMBAI : The Mumbai Cricket Association ( MCA ) is upset with reports that the IPL final , to be held on June 1 , may be shifted out of the Wankhede Stadium to Chennai due to some venue management issues. " Both Sunil Gavaskar ( BCCI president-IPL ) , and Ranjib Biswal ( IPL chairman ) have assured me that the final will be held at the Wankhede , " MCA vicepresident Ravi Savant told the TOI on Wednesday . In fact , it is learnt that the IPL Governing Council ( GC ) may be forced to move out a Qualifier 1 ( May 27 ) and the eliminator ( May 28 ) from Chennai if the teams playing in those games feature Sri Lankan players . Meanwhile , Savant said : " We have a contract with the Mumbai Indians regarding this , so there is no chance that the final will not be held at the Wankhede Stadium. Further , the IPL rules clearly state that the final will be held at the venue of the defending champion.
