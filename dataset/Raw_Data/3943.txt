Marsh to deliver 15th MCC Spirit of Cricket Cowdrey Lecture The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 2 , 2015 , 06.38PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 LONDON : Former Australian cricketer Rod Marsh will deliver the 2015 MCC Spirit of Cricket Cowdrey Lecture at the Lord 's on September 1 . Marsh will become the fourth Australian to give the speech after Simon Taufel ( 2013 ) , Adam Gilchrist ( 2009 ) and Richie Benaud - who gave the inaugural Cowdrey Lecture in 2001 . Marsh , who is Australia 's chief selector , is also a part of the MCC World Cricket committee alongside Kumar Sangakkara , who delivered the Cowdrey Lecture in 2011 . " After Marsh 's address , a question and answer session with him and a selected panel will tackle topics arising from the Lecture and other issues prevalent in world cricket , " according to an MCC release . Other previous MCC Spirit of Cricket Cowdrey Lecturers have included Sunil Gavaskar in 2003 , the Most Reverend Desmond Tutu in 2008 , and Sir Ian Botham in 2014 . Rod Marsh played 96 Tests and 92 One-Day Internationals for Australia during a fourteen-year career . MCC president David Morgan said , " Rod Marsh is a popular and well-respected man in cricket and it is a pleasure to announce him as this year 's MCC Spirit of Cricket Cowdrey Lecturer. This is an extremely important date for MCC and I have no doubt Rod will have some very interesting things to say about our ever-evolving sport.
