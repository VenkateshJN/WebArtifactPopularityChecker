( TOI Photo ) RELATED KOLKATA : Kolkata Knight Riders skipper Gautam Gambhir won the toss and elected to bowl against Sunrisers Hyderabad in an IPL match at Eden Gardens on Saturday . KKR are unchanged while SRH have made two changes , leaving out Aaron Finch and Irfan Pathan to include Jason Holder and Anirudh Srikanth .
