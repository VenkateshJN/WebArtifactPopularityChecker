( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 HOBART : Scotland were dismissed for just 130 by Australia after losing the toss in their World Cup Pool A match at Hobart 's Bellerive Oval on Saturday . Scorecard Left-arm fast bowler Mitchell Starc did the bulk of the damage with four for 14 in 4.4 overs . Scotland 's innings included five ducks and they barely got past the halfway point , the team all out in the 26th over . Full coverage : ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 Four-time champions and tournament co-hosts Australia were already through to the quarter-finals , where they will play an as yet to be decided opponent at the Adelaide Oval on March 20 . For Scotland , who 've yet to win a match at the World Cup in what is now their third appearance -- a run of 13 games -- this was their last match of the tournament .
