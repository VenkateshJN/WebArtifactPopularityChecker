Choukse guides Central Railway to DRM Challenge Cup final The author has posted comments on this article Suhas Nayse , TNN | Mar 14 , 2015 , 11.30AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NAGPUR : Guided by a brilliant half-century by Vaibhav Choukse , DSA Central Railway ( DSACR ) trounced Dr Ambedkar College Sports Academy ( DACSA ) by seven wickets in the second semifinal of the Haier Twenty20 DRM Challenge Cup Invitational Cricket tournament at the Central Railway Ajni ground here on Friday . Opting to bat , DACSA were restricted to 116 for 8 in 20 overs. Ashish Pande struck an unbeaten 44 with the help of 3 sixes and one boundary. Chandan Shah ( 19 ) and Yugandhar Nathe ( 15 ) too batted well. Vikas Yadav and Mangesh Joshi scalped two wickets each . DSA Central Railway reached the target in 15 overs with seven wickets to spare. Vaibhav Choukse 's innings of 51 ( 4x4 , 1x6 ) put the Central Railway right on the track from the beginning. Man-of-the-match Choukse got able support from Anup Fulper ( 38 ) and Sanjog Binkar ( 19 not out ) . DSA Central Rly will now clash with Advocate XI in the final . BRIEF SCORES DACSA 116-8 in 20 overs ( Ashish Pandey 44 , Chandan Shah 19 , Yugandhar Nathe 15 ; Vikas Yadav 2-10 , Mangesh Joshi 2-18 , Shailesh Harbade , Nachiket Parande , Gaurav Upadhyaya 1 wkt each ) lost to DSA Central Railway 119-3 in 15 overs ( Vaibhav Choukse 51 , Anup Fulper 38 , Sanjog Binkar 19 ; Gaurav Dhoble 2-32 , Mayank Sondakar 1-16 ) by 7 wickets .
