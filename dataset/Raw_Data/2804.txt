NBA : Beasley rejoins Heat after Shanghai Sharks stint The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Feb 27 , 2015 , 10.30AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 MIAMI : Michael Beasley rejoined the Miami Heat on Thursday , signing a 10-day contract after completing a season for the Chinese Basketball Association 's Shanghai Sharks earlier this month . Beasley , who was taken by Miami with the second pick of the 2008 NBA Draft , played two seasons with the Heat before being traded to Minnesota and rejoined the team last season , averaging 7.9 points and 3.1 rebounds over 55 games to help the Heat reach the NBA Finals , losing there to San Antonio . The 26-year-old forward 's latest arrival in Miami comes five days after the Heat announced that All-Star forward Chris Bosh would miss the remainder of the NBA season with blood clots in his lungs . Beasley averaged 27.1 points , 10.4 rebounds , 5.2 assists and 1.92 steals in 37 games with the Yao Ming-owned Sharks while shooting 51.3 percent from the floor in his Chinese stay. Shanghai went 17-21 , missing the Chinese league playoffs . Beasley was the Most Valuable Player at the 2015 Chinese league 's All-Star Game , coming off the bench to score a CBA All-Star Game-record 59 points . In 214 career games with Miami , 99 as a starter , Beasley has averaged 12.7 points and 5.2 rebounds . The Heat , ranked third-worst in the NBA in scoring , are 25-31 entering Friday 's game at New Orleans , where Beasley is likely to make his return. Miami is seventh in the Eastern Conference but only 1 1/2 games ahead of Charlotte and Brooklyn and 2 1/2 ahead of Boston in the fight for the two final East playoff berths .
