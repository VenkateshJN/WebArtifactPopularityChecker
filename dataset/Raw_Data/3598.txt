( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 PERTH : England captain Eoin Morgan won the toss and elected to bowl in the final tri-series one-day international against India at the WACA Ground on Friday . The winner of the match will advance to Sunday 's final against Australia in Perth . Seam bowler Mohit Sharma was promoted by India at the expense of paceman Ishant Sharma , who was ruled out due to injury . England named an unchanged side . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( Capt. ) , Ambati Rayudu , Shikhar Dhawan , Ajinkya Rahane , Virat Kohli , Suresh Raina , Ravi Jadeja , Akshar Patel , Stuart Binny , Mohammed Shami , Mohit Sharma . England : Eoin Morgan ( Capt. ) , Ian Bell , Moeen Ali , James Taylor , Joe Root , Ravi Bopara , Jos Buttler , Chris Woakes , Stuart Broad , James Anderson , Steven Finn .
