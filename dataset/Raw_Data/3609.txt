( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 India 's most successful Test captain MS Dhoni on Tuesday announced his retirement from Test cricket. Out of 60 Test matches Dhoni played as captain , India won 27 and lost 18 while 15 of them ended in a draw . " - Kapil Dev " As far as one-day cricket is concerned , Dhoni is one of the greatest captains of all times in India. " - Sourav Ganguly " He is undoubtedly one of the best captains we have ever produced. " - Gundappa Vishwanath " He has that uniqueness to respond to a tough situation with ease.
