( AFP Photo ) Page 1 of 4 HOBART : Australia won the toss and elected to bowl in their second tri-series one-day international against England in Hobart on Friday . Australia , unbeaten in their first two matches , made three changes in their bid for a place in the February 1 final in Perth . David Warner and Shane Watson were both rested due to hamstring soreness , while captain George Bailey was suspended for a second slow over rate breach in less than 12 months . Into the side come Shaun Marsh , Cameron White and Moises Henriques. In Bailey 's absence , Test captain Steve Smith will lead the Australians . England named an unchanged line-up from the team that thrashed India by nine wickets in Brisbane on Tuesday . Teams : Australia - Steve Smith ( capt ) , Aaron Finch , Shaun Marsh , Cameron White , Moises Henriques , Glenn Maxwell , Brad Haddin , James Faulkner , Mitchell Starc , Pat Cummins , Gurinder Sandhu England - Eoin Morgan ( capt ) , Ian Bell , Moeen Ali , Joe Root , James Taylor , Ravi Bopara , Jos Buttler , Chris Woakes , James Anderson , Stuart Broad , Steven Finn .
