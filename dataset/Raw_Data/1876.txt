World Cup 2015 : Dhoni is the best captain India has had , says Bhutia The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Feb 28 , 2015 , 05.08PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 KOLKATA : Terming Mahendra Singh Dhoni as the best captain India have ever produced , former national football team skipper Bhaichung Bhutia on Saturday said the wicketkeeper-batsman has it in him to help the country defend the cricket World Cup title in Australia . " Dhoni is definitely the best captain India have ever had. I think he 's going to win the World Cup this time as well , " an overwhelmed Bhutia said here on the sidelines of a promotional event . " We have won Twenty20 and 50-over World Cups under Dhoni. Hopefully they would do it again. When you start well , you finish well also. Obviously India are the favourites , " the 38-year-old retired India footballer said . The 'Sikkimese Sniper ' , who is currently doing a sports management course in Holland , however admitted that he 's not able to follow every bit of the ongoing World Cup in Australia and New Zealand . " Seriously speaking it 's difficult to analyse , I 've not seen any match so far. I just keep travelling. Today also I could catch up with a couple of ( Indian ) overs at the airport.
