Rajasthan win toss , elect to field against Delhi PTI | May 3 , 2014 , 08.04 PM IST Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to field against Delhi Daredevils. ( PTI Photo ) RELATED NEW DELHI : Rajasthan Royals skipper Shane Watson won the toss and elected to field against Delhi Daredevils in their IPL match at the Feroz Shah Kotla stadium on Saturday . Rajasthan made one change , replacing spinner Iqbal Abdulla with Dhawal Kulkarni , while Delhi went for leg spinner Rahul Sharma in place of Laxmi Shukla .
