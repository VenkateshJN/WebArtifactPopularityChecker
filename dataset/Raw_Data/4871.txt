World Cup 2015 : Warner hits century as Australia dominate The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 4 , 2015 , 02.20PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 PERTH ( Australia ) : Australian opener David Warner went to his first World Cup century as the four-time champions dominated their World Cup clash against Afghanistan at the WACA on Wednesday . He reached the landmark in the 25th over having hit 11 fours as his side reached 151-1 after 25 overs . It was the 27-year-old Warner 's fourth career ODI century .
