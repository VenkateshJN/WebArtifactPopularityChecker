Eden Gardens to host May 27 first IPL qualifier PTI | May 10 , 2014 , 07.27 PM IST READ MORE IPL | Subir Ganguly | Mumbai Indians | Kolkata police RELATED KOLKATA : The IPL governing council awarded the first IPL qualifier on May 27 to Eden Gardens , which lost out on Kolkata Knight Riders ' first home match on May 14 due to security related issues in the wake of Lok Sabha elections . " We have been awarded the first qualifier on May 27 at the emergent governing council meeting , " CAB joint secretary Subir Ganguly , who is also the IPL governing council member , said from Mumbai . He further informed that May 28 's play-off match was also shifted out of Chennai , even as Mumbai will now host the eliminator and the qualifier two on May 30 . The M A Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai was originally scheduled to host the first qualifier and an eliminator in the play-off round but both the matches were shifted after the Chennai Municipal Corporation claimed three stands in stadium were constructed illegally and refused to give No Objection Certificate .
