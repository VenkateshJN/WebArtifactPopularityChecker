World Cup 2015 : MS Dhoni overtakes Sourav Ganguly 's record of most away ODI wins The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 6 , 2015 , 07.46PM IST Under MS Dhoni 's leadership , India won the 2007 World T20 and the 2011 World Cup at home. ( Getty Images ) Page 1 of 4 PERTH : Mahendra Singh Dhoni on Friday surpassed former skipper Sourav Ganguly as the most successful Indian captain in terms of ODI victories achieved on foreign shores . Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 The four-wicket win over the West Indies in the ICC World Cup 2015 here was Dhoni 's 59th while Ganguly had 58 victories under his belt . The current Indian captain played a key role with an unbeaten 45-run knock , helping his side to a hard-fought win at the WACA . Under his leadership , India won the 2007 World T20 and the 2011 World Cup at home , besides the Champions Trophy in England two years ago . He was also at the helm when India achieved the number one Test rankings .
