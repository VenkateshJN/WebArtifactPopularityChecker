( TOI Photo ) RELATED ABU DHABI : Chennai Super Kings won the toss and decided to bat against Delhi Daredevils in the Indian Premier League on Monday . At the toss , Super Kings captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni said West Indies all-rounder Dwayne Bravo has been ruled out for " four to five " weeks as a result of a shoulder injury he suffered in the last game . For Daredevils , star player Kevin Pietersen will miss his third game in a row as he is yet to recover from a finger injury .
