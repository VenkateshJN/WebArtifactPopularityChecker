World Cup 2015 : India win toss and put Zimbabwe into bat The author has posted comments on this article AFP | Mar 14 , 2015 , 08.10AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 AUCKLAND : Indian captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni won the toss and sent Zimbabwe into bat in the World Cup Pool B match at Eden Park in Auckland on Saturday . Scorecard India , who have already qualified for the quarterfinals after winning all their previous five matches , kept the same side which beat Ireland last time out . Zimbabwe , out of the tournament after recording just one win in five matches , are again without their injured captain Elton Chigumbura for a second match running . Brendan Taylor takes over the captaincy for the second successive game in what is likely to be his final international appearance before he takes up a three-year deal to play for English county Nottinghamshire . India will play Bangladesh in the second quarter-final in Melbourne on March 19 . Teams : India : Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( captain ) , Shikhar Dhawan , Rohit Sharma , Virat Kohli , Ajinkya Rahane , Suresh Raina , Ravindra Jadeja , Ravichandran Ashwin , Mohammed Shami , Umesh Yadav , Mohit Sharma Zimbabwe : Brendan Taylor ( captain ) , Sikandar Raza , Regis Chakabva , Tendai Chatara , Chamu Chibhabha , Craig Ervine , Hamilton Masakadza , Solomon Mire , Tawanda Mupariwa , Tinashe Panyangara , Sean Williams Umpires : Bruce Oxenford ( AUS ) and Chris Gaffaney ( NZL ) TV umpire : Rod Tucker ( AUS ) Match referee : Roshan Mahanama ( SRI ) Weather : Clear , with high humidity , Maximum temperature , 27 Celsius .
