Baroda Cricket Association hosts Women 's T20 cricket tournament The author has posted comments on this article Tushar Tere , TNN | Jan 15 , 2015 , 10.33PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 VADODARA : Five teams from the states including West Bengal , Uttar Pradesh , Andhra Pradesh ( AP ) , Maharashtra and Gujarat participated in the Grade C 'Senior women T20 ' plate cricket tournament hosted by Baroda Cricket Association ( BCA ) . The segments for the matches included 'U-14 ' , 'U-19 ' , and senior women. Five test cricket players participated in this tournament that was played at Motibaug cricket ground and Alembic ground . " The T20 women 's cricket is undergoing sea change with more women taking to cricket. With more facilities and encouragement we have the potential to become world champions. The encouragement should start at the school level by hosting training camps to identify and train potential young girls to become future cricketers , " said Hima Bindu , captain of the AP cricket team and member of the national cricket team . " It was great privilege to have players from the national team as part of the tournament , " said Rajkuvar Devi Gaekwad , convener , women cricket committee , Baroda Cricket Association. " Beena Surti who is a senior strike batsman and Tarannum Pathan captain of the Baroda cricket team performed well , " added Gaekwad. ?
