Â© AP London : Yuvraj Singh will play for Marylebone Cricket Club ( MCC ) in the Emirates Twenty20 tournament in Dubai on March 20 . The Indian batsman has been named as the final member of the squad for the annual competition which this year also features Lancashire , Yorkshire and Sussex . Lancashire will take on Yorkshire in the first semi-final and MCC play Sussex in the afternoon , before the final takes place in the evening . Yuvraj will join Alastair Cook , Michael Carberry and Nick Compton in the MCC squad. Nepal captain Paras Khadka will also be part of the MCC side for the Emirates T20 tournament , a release from the MCC said . The Emirates T20 tournament precedes the Champion County match between MCC and Yorkshire. This annual fixture is a day-night match played with a pink ball under the MCC Spirit of Cricket banner , and acts as a curtain-raiser for the new English county season . " I am very excited about being part of the squad for this tournament , and I 'd like to thank MCC for offering me the chance to play out in Dubai again. There are some great players in the squad so we stand a good chance of winning the competition. I 've got fond memories of playing against MCC in the Lord 's Bicentenary match last year and I 'm delighted to be involved with the Club again , " Yuvraj said .
