Dhoni wins toss , opts to bowl against Punjab in IPL Qualifier PTI | May 30 , 2014 , 07.52 PM IST Chennai have fielded an unchanged side while Punjab have made one change , bringing in Sandeep Sharma and dropping Rishi Dhawan in the IPL Qualifier-2. ( PTI Photo ) RELATED MUMBAI : Chennai Super Kings skipper Mahendra Singh Dhoni won the toss and elected to bowl against Kings XI Punjab in the IPL Qualifier-2 at Wankhede stadium , on Friday . Chennai have fielded an unchanged side while Punjab have made one change , bringing in Sandeep Sharma and dropping Rishi Dhawan . The winner of today 's match will clash for the title with Kolkata Knight Riders on Sunday .
