Village of Raina 's fiance takes holiday to watch World Cup semis The author has posted comments on this article PTI | Mar 25 , 2015 , 02.49PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 BAGHPAT ( UP ) : The village of batsman Suresh Raina 's fiance in Bamnauli is gearing up for the ICC World Cup semifinal on Thursday and announced an undeclared holiday as India faces their toughest test taking on a formidable Australia . Head of the Panchayat Kalu Singh said the village is proud of Raina , who set to tie knot to Priyanka Chowdhury a resident of the village . " It is the matter of pride that we are getting son-in-law like Raina. The decision to remain off from respective duties and watch the match , was taken unanimously yesterday. Also the entire village prayed at Hanuman temple for victory of the Indian team , " Singh said . Everyone will watch the semi-final on a big screen which will be installed in the village .
