Full Coverage : ICC World Cup 2015 1. First things first , winning the toss proved to be decisive for India as captain MS Dhoni made the right decision to bat first. After losing the toss , Pakistan captain Misbah-ul-Haq said that he too was looking to bat first . 2. Despite his poor run of late , India kept faith in Shikhar Dhawan and the opener roared back into form by hitting 73 runs off 76 balls . 3. Sending Virat Kohli back at the number three spot and the batsman responded by hitting a brilliant hundred to become the first Indian centurion against Pakistan in the World Cup . 4. Sending Suresh Raina ahead of Ajinkya Rahane after the dismissal of Shikhar Dhawan to keep the left-right combination going as the southpaw smashed 74 runs off 56 balls . 5. Indian bowlers , who have faced some flak recently for their poor performances , brilliantly rose to the occasion to defend the total and they were ably supported by the fielders .
