Surprised Yuvraj not in World Cup team : Harbhajan The author has posted comments on this article TNN | Feb 3 , 2015 , 11.37AM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 NEW DELHI : Off-spinner Harbhajan Singh has said that Yuvraj Singh should have been part of the Indian team for the upcoming World Cup . " I was surprised not to see Yuvraj 's name in the squad. He is a match-winner and I though he had a very good chance of making it , " Harbhajan , who was part of the World Cup winning squad in 2011 along with Yuvraj , said at a cricket conclave on Monday . Yuvraj , when asked if the omission surprised him , said , " I was disappointed. But I have to accept it and keep scoring runs in domestic cricket. " He then praised another India discard , Gautam Gambhir who had played crucial knocks in two finals - World T20 in 2007 and World Cup 2011 . " We would n't have won those tournaments if Gambhir had n't scored those big runs in the finals , " a slimmed-up Yuvraj said. Gambhir , meanwhile , made an interesting point : " If Yuvraj had come to bat ahead of Dhoni in the 2011 World Cup final against Sri Lanka , he would have scored a hundred that day.
