Australia hammer Zimbabwe by 198 runs The author has posted comments on this article AP | Aug 25 , 2014 , 08.29PM IST Share on Linkedin Page 1 of 4 HARARE : Australia made its mark in its first game in Zimbabwe in a decade as it racked up a ground-record score in a huge 198-run win over the home team on Monday to open the triangular one-day series . Some brutal hitting by Australia 's batsmen powered it to 350-6 - the highest ODI total at Harare Sports Club - and the bowlers followed up to dismiss Zimbabwe for 152 in 39.3 overs . Glenn Maxwell smashed 93 from just 46 balls with nine fours and five sixes for Australia. Mitchell Marsh thumped 89 off 83 balls with seven fours and four sixes. They blitzed Zimbabwe 's bowlers in a damaging fourth-wicket stand of 109 from only 54 deliveries . Hamilton Masakadza made 70 for Zimbabwe but had no support. Steve Smith took 3-16 for Australia .
